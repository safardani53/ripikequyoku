School Tales: The Series Is Set To Release On Netflix Soon

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, March 10, 2024

School Tales: The Series, Netflix’s second Thai movie this year, is a horror story. James Thanadol Nuansut, Tum Putipong Saisikaew, Songsak Mongkolthong, and Mike Phontharis Chotkijsadarsopon co-directed the eight-part Netflix original anthology series. The anthology has been adapted from a famous comic book series that focuses on horror at school and has haunting libraries and Soylent Green-style cafeterias, as well as naive kids and terror lurking in their midst. Continue reading to learn more about the new Netflix series School Tales: The Series.

What is the plot of School Tales: The Series? View the trailer here.

Every high school has its own drama, which is frightening in and of itself. But add headless demons, ghosts, and other horrors to the mix, and you have School Tales: The Series. The series, which is dense with horror and drama, follows the horrors that lurk about the school, waiting for the ideal time to strike. Will the kids survive, or will they succumb to the evil side?

Netflix has also issued a series summary, which is as follows:

“Eight terrifying comic book tales have been converted into an eight-part television series that will keep everyone off the school grounds at night: A girl who jumps to her death; a haunting library; canteen food produced from human flesh; a headless ghost in the school warehouse; a devil-infested chamber; a spiteful demon in an abandoned building; and a classroom where only dead pupils attend. Prepare to confront a new kind of terror. The kids own the school throughout the day. It’s a different tale at night.”

Watch the School Tales: The Series trailer here.

The two-minute official trailer, which is full of jump scares, blood, and demons simply serve as a teaser for what is to come in the series. There are creatures, but there also looks to be a book involved, which will likely complicate the plot even more. School Tales: The Series, starring prominent Thai actors Kay Lertsittichai and Mark Siwat Jumlongkul, who may be acquainted with viewers from previous Netflix original movies, promises to be a promising horror anthology that is guaranteed to pique the interest of horror aficionados.

When and how can you see the anthology?

We now have a release date for School Tales: The Series, in addition to the trailer and description. On August 10, 2022, the eight-episode anthology will air. Because it is a Netflix original series, it will only be accessible to view on Netflix. Because Netflix, unlike other streaming services, releases all episodes simultaneously, all eight episodes of the series will be available on Netflix on the planned day of release.

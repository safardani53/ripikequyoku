Kraft brings back previously discontinued item and it's perfect for adding to morning lattes

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

KRAFT has brought back a previously discontinued snack that’s perfect with those morning lattes.

Customers will love the seasonal flavor that comes just in time for the fall.

Kraft has reintroduced its pumpkin spice Jet-Puffed marshmallows as a seasonal item.

These bite-sized pieces add a cozy flavor to the bakery aisle favorite.

The fall variety has been absent all year but customers can now find theirs at retailers nationwide like Target and Walmart.

These Jet-Puffed marshmallows are shaped like pumpkins and have a soft, spongy texture.

That makes them perfect for adding to hot cocoa or your morning latte.

They could also be roasted into S’mores for a pumpkin spice twist on the campfire classic.

At Walmart, an 8oz bag is $4.87 and there are 100 calories in a 12-piece serving.

But Kraft isn’t the only company leaning into the pumpkin spice craze this year.

Krispy Kreme has unveiled its pumpkin spice lineup already, with several items available to order now.

First, the pumpkin spice original glazed donut and pumpkin spice cake donut are both back, giving customers their first autumnal flavors of the year.

Meanwhile, there are two brand-new options for 2023. 

The pumpkin spice cheesecake swirl donut features pumpkin buttercream icing and cream cheese icing, topped with a light dusting of cinnamon sugar.

And the pumpkin spice maple pecan donut offers maple icing topped with crunchy candied pecan pieces.

Coffee lovers can also choose from the pumpkin spice latte, served hot, iced, or frozen, and the pumpkin spice coffee, served hot or iced.

In grocery stores, customers can look out for a donut six-pack featuring the pumpkin spice cheesecake swirl and the pumpkin spice cake varieties.

7-Eleven also began serving up its fall favorite coffees on August 1.

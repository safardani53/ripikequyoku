Kelly Brook flashes her bum and sideboob in a sexy throwback to the Snatch premiere 17 years ago

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 8, 2024

KELLY Brook has treated fans to a very sexy throwback picture – showing her wearing an incredibly racy dress.

The model, now 37, posted a shot of herself from 17 years ago when she attended the premiere of Snatch wearing her most daring dress to date.

Ditching her underwear, Kelly wore the barely there pink dress by Julien MacDonald that reveal her pert bum and impressive boobs.

She cheekily captioned the shot: “Chilly.”

At the time Kelly was dating actor Jason Statham, who played illegal boxing promoter Turkish in the hit film directed by Guy Ritchie.

It’s a far cry from the Kelly we know and love today, who has swapped the glamour for gardening and domestic bliss with Jeremy Parisi.

The former Big Breakfast host  ditched her party lifestyle to live on an apple and pear farm in Kent and she now wants to sex up gardening by launching her own TV show, filled with innuendos and cleavage shots.

She told The Sun: “I love planting shrubs and my lady garden.

“I would love a gardening show, now I’m not doing anymore reality shows. Me and Monty Don would be a good presenting combo.

“Gardening is now my full time hobby and it’s great exercise. I don’t mind getting my hands dirty.”

The move comes after she posted sexy garden snaps on Instagram, which led to a stint on BBC2’s Chelsea Flower Show coverage.

Kelly could be the successor to Charlie Dimmock, 50, who became a sex symbol in the 1990s for gardening braless on hit BBC show Groundforce.

She said: “If I undo a button on my boiler suit I might increase the viewers too.”

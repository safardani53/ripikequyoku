Corliss Williamson was born on a Tuesday, December 4, 1973 in Arkansas. His birth name is Corliss Mondari Williamson and he is currently 50 years old. People born on December 4 fall under the zodiac sign of Sagittarius. His zodiac animal is Ox.

Corliss Mondari Williamson is an American basketball coach and former basketball player who played for four teams during his 12-year NBA career. He currently serves as an assistant coach for the Sacramento Kings. His nickname is “Big Nasty”, a moniker he received from his AAU coach when he was 13. Williamson was a dominating power forward in college, but became an undersized power forward in the NBA and mostly played at the small forward position.

He was a high school McDonald’s All-American in 1992, before attending the University of Arkansas. Under legendary coach Nolan Richardson, Williamson led Arkansas to the 1994 NCAA Championship and took Most Outstanding Player honors.

Who is Nina Warhurst's husband and do they have any children?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, February 26, 2024

NINA Warhurst is riding high as one of the most popular presenters on BBC Breakfast.

But when she's not busy taking over the world of TV, she enjoys a cosy home life with her husband Ted.

Who is Nina Warhurst's husband Ted?

Nina Warhurst is happily married to her husband Ted, who is a caterer.

He runs Fed by Ted which describes itself on Instagram as "Pop-ups & private catering in Manchester & beyond".

He regularly posts delicious-looking food on the social media site @fedbyted1.

In November 2022, Ted revealed that he was making over part of his garden to provide home-grown produce for his dishes.

He also shares pictures advertising his pop-ups on Facebook and Twitter @fedbyteduk.

Ted was born in November 1982.

On his 40th birthday on November 12, 2022, Nina posted: "You are my favourite."

When did Nina and Ted start dating?

Nina and Ted's love story started when they met on holiday in Croatia in 2013.

Do Nina and Ted have any children?

On July 3, 2023, Nina's colleagues Jon Kay and Sally Nugent revealed the wonderful news that had welcomed a baby girl.

Announcing the news to viewers, Sally said: "Some very exciting news now about our colleague Nina Warhurst, who we said goodbye to not very many days ago as she went off on maternity leave.

"She has welcomed a very healthy, beautiful baby girl."

Pictures of the newborn were then shown on screen to BBC Breakfast viewers and Jon added: "And there she is.

"Nina has told us the baby's name is a working progress, but the great news is they're all doing well after what Nina says was brilliant care from the team at St Mary's in Manchester. Isn't she gorgeous?"

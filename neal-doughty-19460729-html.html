Neal Allan Doughty is an American keyboard player, best known as a founding member of the rock band REO Speedwagon and the only member to ever perform on every album. He formed the group in the fall of 1967, with original drummer Alan Gratzer. Although never one of the main musicians of the REO Speedwagon, Doughty wrote or co-wrote some of the band's songs. Songs for which he is the only composer include "Sky Blues" from 1974, "One Lonely Night" from 1984 and "Variety Tonight" from 1987.

Jim Caviezel Biography

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, August 25, 2024

53 years old American actor Jim Caviezel was born as James Patrick Caviezel in Mount Vernon, Washington, United States of America to James Patrick Caviezel, Sr. and Maggie Caviezel. He is an American and is of Slovakian, Swiss and Irish descent. In 1993, Jim met the love of his life Kerri Browitt on a blind date.

CAPTION: Jim Caviezel SOURCE: Instagram

This date was orchestrated by his sister Amy. Kerri is an English teacher and she is also a talented flutist. They began dating then. After being in love for several years, they got married on the 20th of July 1996. They have already been parents three times.

As a child, Jim was an avid basketball player. He joined John F. Kennedy Memorial High School where he also played on the high school basketball team. After graduating from there in 1987, he joined Bellevue College and he continued playing basketball.

CAPTION: Jim Caviezel SOURCE: Instagram

But when he was in his second year, he got a foot injury, and this lead to an end of NBA hope. He then began focusing on acting and he finally earned the role of Airline Clerk in the 1991 movie, My Own Private Idaho. The following year, he appeared in the television series, The Wonder Years. His net worth is said to be around $30 million.

Andy Garca Biography

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 19, 2024

Empire magazine's one of the 100 Sexiest Stars in film history, Andy García is an actor from Cuba. He is also well known in Hollywood as the film director. He is mainly popular in the world for starring the role of Vincent Mancini in an American crime film . Further, he has also acted in Ocean Eleven, alongside George Clooney and Brad Pitt.

Early life

One of the versatile actors in Hollywood, Andy García was born as Andrés Arturo García Menéndez on 12th April 1956 in Havana, Cuba in the mid-1950s.

He was born to Amelie Menéndez and René García Núñez. He was raised in Havana, Cuba till he was five years old and then he moved to Miami, Florida.

His nationality is American as well as Cuban and his ethnicity is mixed.

After Andy's family moved to Miami, Florida, they began a perfume company and a few years later this company turned into a million-dollar franchise. There he got enrolled at Miami Beach Senior High School. He also attended Florida International University.

Personal life

Andy's personal life is kind of private. He is a married man. He got married to his lovely wife Marivi Lorido on the 24th of September 1982. 

Marivi Lorido is also popular in the film industry as a producer. They have been parents four times from this married life. The name of their kids is Daniella, Alessandra, Andres, and Dominik García-Lorido.

Born in 1956, he is now aged 56 and his height is five feet and ten inches tall. 

Career and net worth

He has been active as an actor since the late 1970s. He made his acting debut by starring the role of Pepe in America's first bilingual situation comedy .

In 1979, he was seen portraying the role of Manuel in the TV series .

He made his feature film debut in 1983 by starring the role of Ken in an American comedy film .

His breakthrough role as an actor came in 1990 when he starred the role of Vincent Mancini in the movie The Godfather: Part III, starring  Al Pacino, Joe Mantegna, and George Hamilton.

To date, he has appeared in more than 50 movies and TV series.

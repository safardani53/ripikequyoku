See Pre-Party Photos &amp; More Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, May 1, 2024

Vanderpump Rules star Scheana Shay, 37, married Brock Davies, 31, in a lavish beach ceremony in Cancun, Mexico, on Tuesday, August 23. Prior to the wedding, the newlyweds and friends partied the night away at an over-the-top Welcome White Party, on Sunday, August 21, at Dreams Natura resort. Two days later, on Tuesday, August 23, the lucky couple tied the knot on the beach, which was followed by a fabulous reception and HollywoodLife has EXCLUSIVE photos from all of the festivities! 

Elaborate is an understatement for Scheana and Brock’s Welcome White Party, and the duo spared no expense. Complete with fire dancers, bongo water drums, tables, and swings set inside the shallow pool at the back of the hotel property, the guests danced and celebrated the happy couple. Guests were treated to an open bar of exotic cocktails and tequila shots. An island DJ played themed tunes for the party and there was also a 3D photo booth, which was a hit among the invitees. As far as the menu goes, there was a buffet that included surf and turf, shrimp, and fajitas. 

The couple’s gorgeous wedding ceremony was picture perfect — literally! After the newlyweds said their “I do’s”, they partied the night away at their reception, which took place out back of the Dreams Natura Resort. Click through photos inside their fun-filled pre-party, ceremony, and reception to find out all the details, including which former and current cast members attended! (AMT, LLC)

Erin Lucas Leaving The City

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, January 31, 2024

Whitney Port’s “friend” Erin Lucas will not be returning to MTV’s The City.

The NYU grad, who appeared on The Hills spinoff’s first season, shot some scenes for the upcoming second season but will no longer be on the show.

“It never hurts to try something new, but when you’re born to do something, chances are you’re going to wind up doing it no matter where you detour,” her rep said.

The overdramatic release continued: “The latest proof comes from Erin Lucas, who loved her hosting career and never meant to stray … until The City beckoned.”

“It was nice while it – and her friendship with Whitney Port – lasted.”

“But it was hosting she loved, and it is to hosting she returns, blowing kisses to all as she departs the series for her hosting home turf.”

Note to rep: No one even knows who Erin Lucas is.

Erin Lucas – whose previous hosting credits include mtvU – has received new job offers and is working on other projects, including a video podcast, called “Style Lounge.”

Why the sudden departure? Sources say Lucas was completely uninteresting and MTV wanted to bring in party girls to stir up fake drama had a falling out with Port.

Someone named Samantha Swetra has been brought in to replace Lucas.

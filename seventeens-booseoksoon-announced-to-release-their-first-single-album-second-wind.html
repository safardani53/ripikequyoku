SEVENTEENs BooSeokSoon Announced To Release Their First Single Album SECOND WIND

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, June 9, 2024

Pledis Entertainment has unveiled the specifics of BooSeokSoon’s long-awaited return on January 12. According to their Weverse post, the group’s debut single album, SECOND WIND, will be released on February 6 at 6 p.m. KST. The album will also be available in physical form, with pre-orders commencing on January 13 at 11 a.m. KST.

BooSeokSoon is a SEVENTEEN sub-unit comprised of members Seungkwan, D.K., and Hoshi. The unit name is a combination of their true names: Boo Seung-kwan, Lee Seok-min, and Kwon Soon-young, also known as BSS. The three are recognized for their crazy energy as well as the humorous situations they generate together. In addition, to mark the release of BSS’s first album, their agency has organized a unique offline event on February 12, 8 p.m. KST dubbed SEVENTEEN BSS’s RUNNING TIME: Run to Andromeda.

The event will include three SEVENTEEN members as MCs, D.K., Seungkwan, and Hoshi, who will conduct commentary while displaying their clever emceeing talents. BooSeokSoon will also play their forthcoming title single live in front of their fans. However, given the limits of an offline event, there are a few criteria for those who want to participate.

Everything you need to know about BooSeokSoon’s impending return album and their offline event.

As with other K-pop event applications, there are a few prerequisites and ways to follow in order to apply for this BooSeokSoon event. Anyone who purchased one or more copies of BSS’s SECOND WIND record from the Weverse Shop GLOBAL between January 13 and February 5 is eligible to qualify for the event. There will be 330 winners picked at random from the pool of applications, and they will be revealed on February 7 at 2 p.m. KST. All candidates must have a Weverse account, and both the event application and the album purchase must be done using the same account.

The number of albums ordered from the Weverse Shop GLOBAL enhances an individual’s chances of joining the event, since purchases purchased via other sites do not qualify for the same. If a person cancels their record purchase after submitting their application for the event, their admission into the event is deemed invalid. The event’s winners are not permitted to transfer or sell their tickets to another person. If identified, the participant will be barred from participating in not just this event, but also all future SEVENTEEN activities. More information on the prerequisites for event participants may be found in SEVENTEEN’s Weverse article.

The next physical album by BooSeokSoo will only be available in one edition, which will include the following items:

Outbox, Photobook, CD, Lyric Book, Photocard (2/6 random), Habit Tracker (1/3 random), BSS Card (1/3 random), Sticker, and Folden Poster.


Finally, BooSeokSoon’s album will include a cardholder with its own set of features such as a cardholder, a selfie picture card (2/ 6 random, 3 variations with 2 images in each), and user instructions. Furthermore, depending on the medium/site used to buy the record, each pre-order of the album will come with its unique set of advantages. Following the launch of BooSeok’s album, SEVENTEEN has begun promotions by updating their social network account headlines and profile images to reflect the color schemes of SECOND WIND.

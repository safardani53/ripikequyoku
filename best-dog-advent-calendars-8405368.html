The 13 Best Dog Advent Calendars

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

Advent calendars are such a fun way to count down the holiday season. Historically, each calendar window revealed an image, often from the nativity scene. Today, advent calendars have evolved into more of a gifting opportunity, containing everything from cheese and beauty products to small jewelry or accessories. There are even advent calendars for dogs (and trust us, your pup will love being treated to a surprise each morning).

So what's behind each door? Some are filled with tasty treats while others contain toys, and some have a little of both — keeping you and your pup on your toes as you count down to the big day.

If you missed out on the viral Bark Advent Calendar at Costco or the coveted (and quick to sell out) Trader Joe’s dog advent calendar, there are still plenty of options available for gifting your pooch this holiday season. We rounded up our favorites, including brands like Bocce's Bakery, Bonne et Filou, and DreamBones, as well as an adorable Hanukkah countdown calendar.

Keep reading to discover the best dog advent calendars of 2024.

Himalayan Pet Supply Best Friend's Advent Calendar

Chewy

If your dog is obsessed with Himalayan Dog Chews, the Himalayan Pet Supply Best Friend’s Advent Calendar is a must. This is one of the best advent calendars for dogs because it has clusters of all-natural dog treats behind 24 windows, so you get a lot for the price. On top of that, they’re gluten and lactose-free and, much like the beloved Yak Cheese Dog Chews, can help improve dental health.

Merry Makings Dog Toy Advent Calendar

Petco

If your pup is more motivated by toys, the Merry Makings Dog Toy Advent Calendar from Petco is a great option. This advent calendar for dogs features a 12-day countdown with 12 corresponding holiday dog toys for a fun daily surprise. Plus, it’s currently on sale for 50 percent off, making it a great budget-friendly option, too. 

Merry Makings Hanukkah 8-Nights of Thrills Dog Toy Calendar

Petco

Celebrate the Festival of Lights with this Hanukkah countdown calendar for dogs. The Merry Makings Hanukkah 8-Nights of Thrills Dog Toy Calendar features eight Hanukkah-themed toys for small to medium-sized dogs to keep them busy.

Bonne et Filou Dog Treats Advent Calendar

Amazon

If you're willing to spend a little extra treating your fur baby during the holidays (or if you're shopping for another devoted dog owner), consider splurging on the Bonne et Filou Advent Calendar. This fancy box contains 24 edible surprises, including 12 bones, eight dog-friendly truffles, and four macaron-style biscuits.

Made with human-grade ingredients, the treats are not only safe for dogs but for people too! With festive sprinkles and frosting, they certainly look good enough to eat, which makes the experience all the more fun for you and your pet.

Bocce's Bakery Advent Calendar

Amazon

We love giving Bocce’s Bakery treats to our dogs because they’re elevated, festive, and all-natural. And with this advent calendar, you get a collection of treats that include dental sticks made from apple, spinach, and coconut, and biscuits made from banana, carob, peanut butter, and oat flour — all for $10.

DreamBone Holiday Collection Advent Calendar

Walmart

If your dog's preferred treats are bones, this is the one to get. The Furry Christmas Advent Calendar contains 24 assorted surprises your pet will go wild for this holiday season.

There are eight chicken-flavored candy canes, eight jerky-wrapped sticks, and eight classic mini-bones baked with sweet potato. Each piece is edible, but you'll want to toss out any remaining chunks. Also, the bones should be bigger than your dog's mouth to prevent choking, so this advent calendar might not be a good option for larger dogs.

SmartBones Holiday Chews

Amazon

The SmartBones Holiday Advent Calendar is one of the best advent calendars for dogs on Amazon. This calendar is based on a classic chocolate advent calendar with 24 count-down boxes — only it features an impressive collection of treats, bones, and rawhides.

Merry & Bright Holiday Dog Advent Calendar

Petsmart

For a mix and treats and toys, consider the Merry & Bright Holiday Dog Advent Calendar. This adorable option from Petsmart comes with nine holiday-themed dog toys and three bags of treats for a 12-day countdown your pup will surely sit and shake for.

Good 'n' Fun 12 Days of Dogmas Holiday Calendar

Target

On a budget? The 12 Days of Dogmas Calendar is an excellent choice that won't break the bank. As the name suggests, it contains 12 days worth of chewy treats dogs love. You'll get eight twist sticks and four kebabs, each packed with natural ingredients and meaty flavors.

We're talking beef, pork, chicken, and duck sourced from real animal hide and liver. Just a heads up, these treats contain wheat flour, so they're not good for gluten-free households or pets with wheat allergies,

Anotion Christmas Dog Toys Advent Calendar

Amazon

For the best dog toys advent calendar on Amazon, consider the Anotion Christmas Dog Toys 12 Days Dog Advent Calendar. This advent calendar is designed for a 12-day countdown and features festive and durable dog toys, such as a rope ball, frisbee, football, and more.

Merry & Bright Frosted 25-Day Advent Calendar for Dogs

Petsmart

If your pooch loves frosted dog cookies, consider the Merry & Bright Frosted 25-Day Advent Calendar for Dogs from Petsmart. The festive holiday advent calendar comes with 24 miniature dog cookies, each decorated to resemble a Christmas tree, snowman, elf, and snowflake, plus one large reindeer cookie for a Christmas morning treat.

Wüfers Dog Cookie Advent Calendar

Amazon

If you have biscuits in mind for your pup, the Wüfers Advent Cookie Calendar might be your best bet. It's filled with 24 fresh-baked treats in fun bone and paw shapes you and your dog will love.

All biscuits contain high-quality, human-grade ingredients sourced locally from the Canadian bakery that makes them. While the ingredients list is considered safe for dogs, they're made with whole wheat flour, so this might not be a great choice for gluten-free households.

Midlee Christmas Tree Advent Calendar for Dogs

Target

Whether your dog has food allergies or you want something you can reuse year after year, this reusable advent calendar for dogs is a great option. The Midlee Christmas Tree Advent Calendar for Dogs is a felt wall hanging with 25 pockets that you can stuff with your pup’s favorite treats while counting down to December 25. While the pockets are a tad too small for toys, you can still get creative with a variety of bones, treats, and frosted cookies, such as the Merry & Bright Snow Many Snacks Cookie Dog Treats.

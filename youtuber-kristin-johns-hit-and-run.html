YouTuber Kristin Johns stands for first time as husband Marcus takes steps with walker after near fa

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, April 1, 2024

YOUTUBER Kristin Johns stood for the first time as husband Marcus used a walker after they were injured in a near-fatal hit-and-run on Monday.

The couple took their first steps since the "extremely traumatic experience," which saw cops chase a suspect across Los Angeles.

Kristin and Marcus sustained broken bones and were rushed to Cedars-Sinai Medical Center when the vehicle mowed them and another pedestrian down in Toluca Lake.

Eye witnesses described the chilling screams of Kristin as she lay crumbled on the ground with "pieces" of her bike around her.

The witness told The Sun: "I saw the aftermath and it was horrible. Kristin was screaming and crying on the ground and they were both clearly in pain not able to move. The car was gone and someone else called 9-1-1 by the time I got there but the bike was in multiple pieces and part of the car was shattered on the ground."

Cops confirmed to The Sun that they have no suspects as the vehicle escaped the LAPD after a brief pursuit near Universal Studios.

The social media influencers were rushed to hospital following the collision, which they believe was intentional, and both underwent surgery after sustaining injuries to their legs.

Kristin and and Marcus shared an update with their fans on Instagram as they took their first steps since the accident.

He wrote: "I’m waking with a walker! Your prayers are working! @kristinjohns did her physical therapy today and was able to sit up. The doctors said that this is actually very good for next day after a femur surgery ...

"Pain for both of has been up and down. Best and worst it’s been so far. Just taking a day at a time."

And Kristin added: "This afternoon I was able to take about 5 steps which is CRAZY! I am so motivated to get up & walk with a walker because then we can go home & be with family."

The pair also said they believed the suspect crashed into them on purpose.

Marcus explained more about the accident in detail earlier this week and said they believe that the person who hit them was trying to flee police following a burglary and purposely "tried to kill them".

The 27-year-old shared: "Me and @kristinjohns are ok! Nothing is official yet, but this is what we think has happened: We were on the far side of the road with other bikers and pedestrians.

"It is a residential neighborhood with multiple stop signs and speed bumps back to back over a short distance.

Adding: "I look down the road and heard an engine revving all the way up and it seemed like in the moment the guy was trying to hit us on purpose.

"I said to Kris 'what is this guy doing!?' He was driving erratically and swerving towards us. I tried to swerved out of the way but he just followed us to hit us."

"I blacked out until I got to the hospital. So after I don’t know what really happened."

Marcus, who explained that the couple hope to be walking again in four months, said he was "unresponsive on the concrete" until the ambulance arrived.

"Sadly Kristin was fully conscious and had to watch me unresponsive on the concrete till ambulance arrived for us," he continued.

"Kristin said we flew about 50 feet from the impact.

He shared: "When I woke up and was trying to explain what happened, and I didn’t think people would believe me that I was convinced he was trying to actually kill us.

"But now it all makes sense if the robbery and police pursuit is officially confirmed. Anyways, scary stuff."

The social media star then asked their followers to "keep us in your prayers" and is glad they are now safe.

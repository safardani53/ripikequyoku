Peter Sellers is an English film actor, comedian and singer. He starred in the BBC comedy series The Goon Show, appeared in a number of hit comedy songs, and is known around the world through numerous film roles including Chief Inspector Clouseau in the series The Pink Panther. Born in Portsmouth, Sellers made his theatrical debut at the Kings Theater in Southsea when he was just two weeks old. He began accompanying his parents in a variety that was touring in provincial theaters. He first worked as a drummer and toured in the UK as a member of the National Service Association Entertainments. He developed his skills in imitation and improvisation while joining the wartime entertainment group of Ralph Reader, the Gang Show, which toured the UK and the Far East. After the war, Sellers debuted his radio station in ShowTime, and he eventually became a regular artist on various BBC radio shows.

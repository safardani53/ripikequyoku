Teen Mom Jenelle Evans slammed for showing off 'weed rolling tray' to her mom Barb after past drug a

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, February 11, 2024

JENELLE Evans is being criticized by fans for flaunting a "weed rolling tray" to her mother Barbara, despite the reality star's past substance abuse.

The fired Teen Mom 2 star has not been shy about her marijuana usage on her TikTok profile, and fans are less than pleased.

Jenelle, 29, posted a controversial TikTok yesterday where she flaunted a new smoking accessory.

The Teen Mom alum showed off a bright green "rolling tray" covered in colored stickers and glitter.

While holding the item in the camera, she asked Barbara, 68: "Do you like my rolling tray mom?"

Barbara nodded her head in agreement as Jenelle urged: "Isn't it nice?"

"Oh yeah, it's got marijuana leaves on it," the grandmother agreed while her daughter stifled a laugh.

Fans were not keen on the video, following Jenelle's past addiction to the substance.

One slammed her public usage, commenting: "Jenelle is really pushing this stoner content lately" while others joked at her childlike behavior writing: "Imagine showing this craft to your mom as a grown a** adult."

Others trolled the mom of three for lacking character, trolling: "Smoking weed doesn't equate a personality" and "Weed is not a personality Jenelle."

Some referenced Barbara's attempts at keeping a positive relationship with her family, adding: "She definitely bites her tongue a lot around them for the sake of keeping peace."

The young mom was also attacked for decorating the tray with weed stickers, as fans previously called her "tacky" and "immature" for using the decoration.

The ex MTV star shared similar content lately, as she finds comedy in involving her mom in her public drug use.

Jenelle and her husband David Eason, 32, reenacted an iconic scene from an early Teen Mom episode where the TV personality was scolded by Barbara for smoking with her ex-boyfriend.

In the old clip, Barbara yelled at Jenelle and her ex Kieffer: "high, high you're both high."

In a new rendition, Barbara partook in the fun by reprimanding her daughter and son-in-law for the sake of Jenelle's TikTok following.

The grandmother approached Jenelle and David while they sat smoking on the shed porch, nagging: "you're both high!"

She reembodied the infamous moment, adding: "High, high, high- you're both high. What the heck is that all about?"

At the end of the video, Barbara urged viewers to help Jenelle get to one million followers on the platform.

Jenelle and her mom have had a tumultuous relationship since her marriage to David in 2017.

Barbara sincerely disagreed with her daughter' decision to tie the knot with David, saying that Jenelle's husband has "so much control over her."

Despite their disagreements, they reunited for the holidays, as Jenelle encouraged her followers to "keep in touch with their loved ones."

The reality star posted an emotional message about her mother to Instagram, writing: "I want to thank all of you for showing my mom some love on the Christmas photos.

"She's been having such a rough week because my uncle, her brother, recently passed away. Thanks for the positivity. It's much needed."

She continued: "With that being said, please don't let years pass before you decide to visit family.

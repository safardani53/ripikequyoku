Ola Jordan says she's quit making sexy calendars as she's 'too old'

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, June 15, 2024

IT’S all Ola for this Strictly dancer — as Ola Jordan reveals she’s quit sexy calendars.

The Polish beauty, 36, admitted she feels too old to do them despite Kelly Brook, 38, and Kylie Minogue, 50, vowing to carry on.

Ola, married to fellow former Strictly Come Dancing professional James, 40, says she’s leaving it to younger rivals.

She said: “No calendar this year, I’m too old now, I’m retiring. I’ve had a lot of messages from disappointed fans which is sweet, but you know I’m getting older now.

“It’s up to the Love Island lot to do it now. I’ve given it up.

Who Is The Voice 2022s (Season 22) Wealthiest Coach?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, January 1, 0001

The Voice is returning for a new season with new coaches. The premiere episode of Season 22 of the singing reality program will debut on Monday, September 19, 2022, at 8 p.m. ET. While coaches Blake Shelton and John Legend will return, they will be joined on the program by newbie Camila Cabello and former coach Gwen Stefani. These trainers are highly sought-after talents in their respective industries, and their skill has earned them millions of dollars. Continue reading to find out who the wealthiest coach in the singing competition is.

The rumored net worth of The Voice 2022 coaches has been explored ahead of the premiere.

Shelton and Stefani will compete as coaches on The Voice for the first time as husband and wife.

1) Gwen Stefani

According to Celebrity Net Worth, American popstar and fashion designer Stefani is the wealthiest coach on The Voice 2022, with a net worth of $160 million. The Rich Girl singer-songwriter first appeared on the show in season 7 in 2014. It was then that she met her future husband, Blake. The next season, she was replaced by Christina Aguilera, but she returned in season 9 and subsequently in season 10 as a fight adviser for Shelton’s side.

Miley Cyrus replaced her in season 12 and she did not return to The Voice until season 17. She then returned in season 19 and won the competition with her artist Carter Rubin.

2) Blake Shelton

The net worth of the American country music artist and television personality is estimated to be about $100 million. He has won the most titles as a The Voice coach, with eight, including Jermaine Paul (Season 2), Cassadee Pope (Season 3), Danielle Bradbery (Season 4), Craig Wayne Boyd (Season 7), Sundance Head (Season 11), Chloe Kohanski (Season 13), Todd Tilghman (Season 18), and Cam Anthony (Season 20).

3) John Legend

John Legend’s net worth is reported to be $100 million USD. He is an American singer-songwriter, actor, producer, and philanthropist. This is his net worth in combination with his wife, supermodel Chrissy Teigen, who has made at least $10 million each year from modeling, television projects, and endorsements since 2018. Legend won season 16 of the singing competition with his artist Maelyn Jarmon on May 21, 2019.

4) Camila Cabello

Camila Cabello, an American singer, has an estimated net worth of $18 million. She came to fame as a member of the girl band Fifth Harmony. Later, she launched her incredibly successful solo career. Camila will compete in the singing competition for the second time. The singer has also competed in the past. She was a mentor to John’s crew. However, this will be her first season as a full-time judge, replacing singer Kelly Clarkson on the panel.

After 21 seasons, The Voice, one of NBC’s most profitable TV series of all time, is returning with season 22 to launch future superstar singers. Tune in to NBC on Monday to see the Season 22 launch of The Voice alongside your favorite judges. The program, which has won eight Emmys so far, is also available on Sling TV, fuboTV, Hulu With Live TV, AT&T TV, and YouTube TV. Peacock offers on-demand episodes of the program.

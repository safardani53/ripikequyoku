Today's Savannah Guthrie shares rare new photos of husband and kids while rocking wild outfit at NFL

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, May 19, 2024

TODAY host Savannah Guthrie has given fans a rare glimpse of her family as she attended an NFL game with them over the weekend.

Savannah, 51, took to Instagram and shared some snaps from her day at the Lincoln Financial Field football stadium.

The TV star, her husband Michael Feldman, and their two children, Vale and Charles, were dressed in support of the Philadelphia Eagles as they played the San Francisco 49ers.

In the photo, Savannah wore a football jersey with a matching woolen hat and kept warm in a black puffer coat and black ankle boots.

Michael, 54, also wore a jersey - which was personalized with his surname - and teamed his with a pair of blue jeans as he posed for photos with his son and daughter.

Vale, eight, and Charles, six, were both wearing football jerseys, too, and appeared to be very excited to be at the game.

Savannah shared a photo of a box of bright green donuts, which her kids were later seen digging into.

The family appeared to have the best seats in the house as they were watching the game from a private box, and Savannah even shared a short video of Charles cheering the players on.

Their family day out comes just days after Savannah embarrassed her husband on live TV.

Last week, as Savannah and her Today co-hosts were talking about a loud noise, she decided to reveal a shocking bedroom secret about her spouse.

On Friday's show, Savannah, Craig Melvin, and Hoda Kotb talked about how an asteroid the size of a truck nearly hit Earth.

The asteroid came within 22,000 miles of the earth's surface.

"This happened last night, by the tip of South America," Craig, 43, explained.

"Scientists are calling it 'one of the closest approaches to Earth ever recorded,' but they added there was no impact because NASA's Impact Assessment System, yes, that is a thing, it's called SCOUT, had ruled out a direct hit."

"But while you were sleeping last night...." Craig said.

Savannah then delivered a jab at her husband by saying: "I think I heard it. Or it was probably snoring next to me."

Hoda, 58, laughed and Craig said: "That sounds about right."

Savannah then laughed and moved on to the next topic.

But just hours later, the couple got all dressed up to enjoy a Friday night out with friends.

Savannah shared a sultry photo alongside Michael from their evening out on the town.

The star took to her Instagram Stories on Friday to post snaps of her over-the-top disco attire for a themed party.

Savannah sported a floor-length purple sequin gown that showed off her curves.

She finished off the look with a faux fur white fluffy jacket and platform heels.

The TV personality posed next to her husband who was decked out in his own Studio 54-esque attire.

The PR consultant wore a sparkly purple shirt, flared jeans, and platform boots of his own for his eclectic costume.

Selena Gomez &amp; More Stars At Taylor Swifts Concert On Eras Tour Hollywood Life

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, March 10, 2024
  • Taylor Swift kicked off her ‘Eras Tour’ in Glendale, Arizona on March 17, with the first leg continuing through summer 2023.
  • Emma Stone, Laura Dern, JJ Watt, and other celebrities have seen her perform on the tour.
  • Channing Tatum, Mindy Kaling, Sofia Vergara, Selena Gomez, Karlie Kloss and more stars attended the Los Angeles shows.

Taylor Swift, 33, has a lot of famous friends, so it’s not surprising that major Hollywood stars got tickets to see her in concert on her Eras Tour. Taylor kicked off her tour in Glendale, Arizona on March 17 and Emma Stone was there dancing in the audience supporting the superstar singer. Kelsea Ballerini even stopped her own concert to ask about the opening night set list on the Eras Tour. Taylor’s Las Vegas show brought out even more celebs, including Ellen Pompeo, Emma Roberts, and Shania Twain.

But the biggest and best celebrity turnout has been at the SIX Los Angeles shows on the Eras Tour. Channing Tatum, Jenna Dewan, Sofia Vergara, and more stars saw Taylor perform in LA. Taylor’s tour, which is in support of all ten of her studio albums and is occurring after the release of 2022’s Midnights, has been extended with more shows in the U.S. in the Fall of 2024. But before that, Taylor will take her tour to other countries like Mexico and Brazil.

There’s been a growing list of celebrities who have made appearances since day one and there’s bound to be more. See which stars have attended the tour here!

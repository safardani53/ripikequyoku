Anna Faris' Upgraded Engagement Ring From Chris Pratt: All the Details

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, April 6, 2024

Blinding — but we’re not mad at it! Chris Pratt and Anna Faris put on an adorable display of PDA at the L.A. premiere of Passengers on Wednesday, December 14, where the Mom actress also debuted a big, brand-new diamond from her husband.

“He just got me this ring. I know. Like two days ago,” Faris, 40, told E! News while admiring her fresh bling. “I look [at it] and I’m like, ‘I can’t believe it either!’ He’s an incredibly romantic man and I’m very, very lucky.”

But Pratt, 37, chimed in that the generosity goes both ways: “She got me a tractor, so we’re kind of even!”

The House Bunny actress’ upgraded ring boasts a whopping center stone surrounded by smaller diamonds with a diamond-studded band. And there is a sentimental touch too: This bauble boasts an oval center stone, just like the original solitaire Pratt gave Faris when he proposed in 2008.

Diamond expert Michael O’Connor gives Stylish the scoop. “Anna’s ring incorporates three of the hottest trends in engagement rings: unexpected center shapes, a halo and micro-pavé style,” he explains. “The micro-pavé is a technique that makes the entire setting shimmer with tiny set diamonds in platinum or white gold to camouflage the metal. The halo has a special meaning — it says ‘You’re the center of my universe’ — and it’s one of the most popular trends right now.”

As for that gigantic diamond, O’Connor adds, “The center oval diamond is of magnificent quality, color and size. It’s about 6 carats, which would make the approximate value of the ring $250,000. Definitely a holiday sparkler!”

Who is Michael Blackson?

Well, his actual name is Jafari Ferguson. The 46-year-old comedian and father of three was born in Ghana and previously engaged to a woman named Georgia Reign. However, he broke things off due to cheating on her end.

Professionally, he has a few acting credits under his belt. He appeared in films like Next Friday, One Night In Vegas, Internet Dating, and Meet The Blacks. He's also worked with stars like Sean "Diddy" Combs, Dave Chappelle, and B.o.B. 

As of now, he's currently in the process of filming the Coming to America sequel, Coming 2 America.

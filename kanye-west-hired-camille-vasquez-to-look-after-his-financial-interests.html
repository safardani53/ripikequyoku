Kanye West Hired Camille Vasquez To Look After His Financial Interests

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, May 22, 2024

Kanye West has allegedly recruited Camille Vasquez of the Brown Rudnick legal firm to manage his “commercial interests.” The attorney and other attorneys, according to TMZ, would represent the rapper’s “contracts, agreements, and works.” However, netizens were doubtful about Camille Vasquez’s supposed hiring, believing that even the celebrity lawyer would be unable to perform miracles for Kanye:

Why did Kanye West hire Camellie Vasquez?

Vasquez’s selection in the aftermath of West’s recent scandals drew much criticism. Earlier this month, the Donda founder was chastised for wearing a “White Lives Matter” t-shirt to the Yeezy presentation during Paris Fashion Week. Soon after, he made many anti-Semitic statements on social media, prompting the suspension of his Instagram and Twitter accounts. West has also been sued for $250 million for making inflammatory remarks concerning the death of George Floyd.

During an appearance on Drink Champs, the singer claimed Floyd died as a result of a fentanyl overdose rather than Derek Chauvin’s physical attack. The rapper’s words generated even more uproar. Following the incidents, Balenciaga announced the termination of all relations with the West. According to a brand statement:

“Balenciaga no longer has any affiliation with this artist, nor any intentions for future collaborations including this artist.”

West also had a falling out with Gap and ended his contract with the company before Paris Fashion Week, while also criticizing Adidas. Needless to say, the rapper hired a legal team, including Camille Vasquez, to investigate his company in the midst of his continuous problems. Vasquez rose to prominence after defending Johnny Depp in his defamation action against Amber Heard. The lawyer was a social media phenomenon after playing a significant part in Depp’s triumph.

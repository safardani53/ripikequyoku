Aaron Chalmers stops fellow Newcastle fighter Ash Griffiths in stunning first round win at Bellator

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, April 22, 2024

AARON CHALMERS kept up his 100 per cent record with an incredible first-round stoppage over Ash Griffiths.

The Geordie Shore-turned-MMA star pulled off the stunning win on the biggest stage of his promising fight career at the SSE Wembley to take his total to 4-0.

Chalmers, 30, once again silenced his critics with a spectacular standing guillotine choke in front of a packed audience at Bellator 200.

With UFC star Leon Edwards and his BAMMA brother Fabian on his team, Chalmers had been working on his ground work after winning his last two bouts with his fists.

Though his right hand helped him out here, clocking Griffiths in the face and sending him tumbling to the floor.

But Griffiths started the better of the two fighters as he pinned Chalmers against the cage before stunning him with a brutal knee to the face followed by a huge right hook.

It simply made the ex-reality TV star angry - and he responded with a furious one-two combo which sent Griffiths sprawling.

He seized the opportunity and started pounding Griffiths before expertly getting him into a standing guillotine choke until his arms went limp.

Chalmers' celebrity status has seen him mocked by fellow fighters and die-hard MMA fans.

He played up to that role by walking out to 50 Cent's Window Shopper, with the rap legend sitting ringside.

Screaming Geordie Shore alumni Charlotte Crosby, Scotty T and Sophie Kasaei also raised the roof and surely got Chalmers' blood flowing.

Does the former Ex On The Beach star care?

Speaking after the fight, he roared at his doubters: "F*** you!"

Idris Elba buffs up for sexy topless scenes in upcoming Luther movie

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, February 26, 2024

GETTING your kit off is daunting for even the most established actor, especially those rumoured to be in the running for 007.

TV heart-throb Idris Elba has elbowed away the junk food and asked his personal chef to make him look the “bee’s knees” for his upcoming topless scene in the Luther movie.

King The Chef, aka King Enekeme, revealed in an exclusive chat just how he’s whipping the hunk into shape to ensure there isn’t an ounce of fat on him.

He said: “I got him nice and lean — the ladies will love him. Idris wanted to lean up in particular for that film because there’s a topless scene he’s got to do, so he was like, ‘I’ve got to look the bee’s knees’.

“We put him on smoothies, lots of greens, kale and spinach, all that stuff, and avoided junk food.

“Everybody wants a good-looking plate of food. However, you’ve got to be realistic. If you want to get down to this weight in the next two to four weeks, it’s not only going to be dazzle dazzle.

Liz's gone mainstream

AFTER decades of being draped in luxury fashion, Liz Hurley has gone mainstream by collaborating with Karen Millen.

The actress is famed for her 1994 Versace safety-pin dress.

A Karen Millen source said: “Elizabeth is a national treasure and the brand is excited by this high-profile deal.”

It’s cost the firm £300,000 but it’s worth it to bag Liz, who still looks fab in a bikini.

Let’s hope they do a budget version of that dress.

Brett's break

THEY were one of TV’s funny couples, but we hear that Ted Lasso star Brett Goldstein has split from his comic girlfriend Beth Rylance.

The British actor and comedian, who plays footie captain Roy Kent in the Apple TV show, started dating fellow Brit Beth last year.

But a source tells us they’ve called it off and no longer follow each other on social media.

Our mole reveals: “They’re both in exciting times in their careers and really busy and they just couldn’t make it work.”

We’re sure Brett will have plenty of admirers when season three drops in a few months.

THEY WERE ALL YELLOW ERM, GREEN

DETERMINED to be the most environmentally friendly band on the planet, Coldplay returned home for the British leg of their world tour with a set powered entirely by eco-friendly energy.

The Yellow hitmakers took Wembley by storm last night – with a surprise appearance from Craig David to boot – for the first of their five gigs there.

In a bid to not harm the environment, static bikes, a kinetic dance floor and a cooking oil-powered generator were used to provide all the electricity.

The band split their show into five acts, starting off with recent hits Music Of The Spheres and Higher Power, and saving their classics – Clocks, Viva La Vida and A Sky Full Of Stars – for later.

Frontman Chris Martin also made fan Tokay Aderiye’s night by dedicating the hit single Fix You to her cousin, who is in hospital following a stroke.

Tokay said: “It’s amazing they did that after seeing my sign. It means so much.”

As the band drew their set to an end, Craig David came on stage for a mash-up of his songs Live In The Moment and Fill Me In.

Chris even polished his angelic halo by helping cleaners tidy up after the sold-out gig.

Can he get any more wholesome?

'Just my knee'

SARAH MILLICAN has confessed she once sent a lover a picture of her knee – pretending it was her bottom – when he asked for nude snaps.

She said: “A previous partner wanted me to send a nude and I sent a picture of the crack of my knee with my legs shut so it looked like an a**e crack and he got all aroused and I had to go, ‘Ah it’s just my knee’.”

Sarah told podcast That’s Enough Already With Urzila Carlson: “I could get somebody to sketch it then send it on as it might be a more pleasing visual than an actual photo.”

Maybe she’ll become a life drawing model.

RACHEL IS HITTING THE DECKS

THE age-old advice to get over heartbreak is to get under someone else.

But Rachel Stevens has decided to go her own way and make over her career instead.

Last month the former S Club 7 singer split from Alex Bourne, her husband of 12 years, saying they were no longer in love with each other.

Now we can reveal she is in talks to be a DJ on Heart Radio and will be on air soon.

A source said: “Bosses are keen to get Rachel on staff and she sees it as the fresh start she needs after her marriage breaking down.

"Nothing better to distract someone from their heartbreak than work.”

Amanda Holden, Emma Bunton and Kelly Brook are already Heart DJs and Rachel will join them from next month to cover presenters’ holidays.

Hopefully, her ex Alex is not a fan of the station, otherwise things might get awkward.

JO GIG'S A DREAM

JO O’MEARA, Boisdale of Canary Wharf, East London

FORMER S CLUB 7 frontwoman Jo O’Meara treated fans to an intimate acoustic set in London’s banking district.

Jo showed off her incredible vocal range performing covers including Bryan Adams' Heaven, Aretha Franklin's A Natural Woman and Whitney Houston's Greatest Love Of All.

But it was Fleetwood Mac's Dreams that held the greatest meaning for the songstress, who told us: “It’s the song that landed me the job in the band.”

And the reason she chose it? “Because it’s an easy song to sing.”

Only if you have lungs like Jo.

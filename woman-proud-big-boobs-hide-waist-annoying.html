I'm proud of my big boobs but they hide the smallest part of my waist, it's so annoying

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 1, 2024

A WOMAN with one of the biggest pairs of boobs in Britain reckons her natural bust is better than anything surgery could give her.

Rachel Aldana, 35, wears a 32LL bra and admits she suffers due to the weight she's carrying sometimes - as each breast weighs over one stone.

But the glamour model, who has been married for over a decade, has learned to ignore the stares and embrace her body after turning down reduction surgery.

She says: "I prefer natural big boobs, they are real, soft, squishy and bouncy and move. Plus a natural hang is always better in my eyes but each to their own.

"The only good thing about having big boobs for me is the money it earns me. It allows me to have this industry as a job.

"The negative is it hides my smallest part of my waist, makes me hot when busy and gives me the worst back ache.

"I squeeze into bras that are high street and I can never shop online for clothes as I have to try everything on.

"I don’t make eye contact with anyone to even know if women get angry but I know I get stared at as I can feel the stares.

"It really doesn’t bother me, I’m too long in the tooth and used to it to care."

But Rachel, from Brackley, Northamptonshire, also tries to prevent any negatives by looking after her assets.

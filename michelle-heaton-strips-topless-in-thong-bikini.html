Michelle Heaton strips topless in thong bikini as she gets a tan top up after Dancing On Ice

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, February 27, 2024

MICHELLE Heaton stripped off as she flashed the flesh to get a tan top-up following her participation in Dancing on Ice.

The singing star, 43, bared all as she posed in a barely-there thong bikini in the striking snap.

Positioning her back to the camera to have her final tan top-up for Dancing on Ice's final, the Liberty X singer showed off her assortment of tattoos as well as her golden tan.

Revealing she wasn't happy to be waving goodbye to getting her fake tan every week for the contest, Michelle captioned the snap: "All tanned up thanx to @fakebake_uk @fakebakeofficial who's been with us all 9 weeks @dancingonice going to miss this... #blessed."

Michelle flashed her pearly whites at the camera as the happiness radiated off her face about having a summer look for the final of the ITV contest.

The star's fans flocked to the comments section to heal the praise on a nearly nude Michelle with one writing: "Looking fab hun love your ink xx."

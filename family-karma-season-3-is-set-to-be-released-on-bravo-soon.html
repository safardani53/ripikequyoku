Family Karma Season 3 Is Set To Be Released On Bravo Soon

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, March 28, 2024

Bravo unveiled the teaser for Family Karma Season 3 at BravoCon on Saturday, October 15, and fans are ecstatic. The series will debut on Bravo on November 6 at 9 p.m. ET. Fans may also catch up on the episodes via the Peacock streaming app. The show’s upcoming season will include two weddings, probable romances, and, of course, sharp one-liners from aunties. Family Karma Season 3 will include Amrit Kapai, Anisha Ramakrishna, Bali Chainani, Brian Benni, Monica Vaswani, Rish Karam, and Vishal Parvani.

What can we anticipate from Season 3 of Family Karma?

As Vishal Parvani and Amrit Kapai explain in the teaser, Family Karma Season 3 will include two weddings.

“We’re marrying — but not to each other!”

To achieve their “ideal Bollywood ending,” Amrit will marry Nicholas Kouchoukos, while Vishal will marry Richa Sadana. Amrit and Nicholas will also consider having children in the next season. The camera will also accompany Anisha Ramakrishna on her mission to marry her ideal “mystery guy.” Last year, Anisha was photographed freezing her eggs. When asked about her engagement, she said,

“I am overjoyed with my fiancé, and we have our parents’ blessings, which is what is most important to me.”

She will not tell the aunties about her boyfriend till she gets wedded to him. Her mother wishes for her to be more private. Monica will be shown attempting to get serious with Rish, but her pals may expose a major truth to the pair, destroying everything. Anisha states in the video,

“The tea has spilled, and I’ve received third-degree burns.”

Bali will try to strike a balance in her life as a mother, O’Malley’s partner, and auntie friend. Brian will make an effort to locate a romantic companion.

Season 3 cast of Family Karma

Mr. Amrit Kapai

Amrit Kapai is a 35-year-old lawyer who works as a senior associate with Goldstein & McClintock LLLP. He graduated from Emory University with a bachelor’s degree in history and economics and from Washington University with a law degree. He came out to his family at the age of 25 and had been dating Nicholas for ten years before they decided to marry.

Anisha Ramakrishna

37-year-old Anisha attended the University of Miami Herbert Business School. She spent several years in the fashion industry before launching her own company, Currently, in 2017. She now hosts her own podcast, Currently Cringing.

Chainani Bali

46-year-old Bali works as a sales and marketing executive for Equiflor Corporation and spends a lot of time with the aunties. She has been divorced twice and now lives as an empty nester with her college-aged daughter. The series description implies that her spouse is pressuring her to make a choice against her will.

Brian Benni

29-year-old Brian works in IT Consulting at SAP and has previously worked as an account executive. He earned a bachelor’s degree in biology from Florida State University. This season, he will be shown leaving his parents’ home as a single guy.

Monica Vaswani

29-year-old Monica is a technology expert and aspiring Bollywood choreographer. On Family Karma, she is shown as the ideal Indian girl, adhering to traditional Indian practices and improving in her relationship with Rish.

Mr. Rish Karam

27-year-old Rish hails from a conventional, business-oriented Indian family. He is presently self-employed as a restaurant. He has known Monica since childhood, but they have just lately begun dating.

Vishal Parvani

Vishal graduated from Florida International University and has been a commercial sales and leasing agent since 2012. The 34-year-old real estate agent is recognized as the life of the party and has completed more than 70 million deals. Season 3 of Family Karma will premiere on Bravo on November 6 at 9 p.m. ET.

Is Bill Whitaker married? | The US Sun

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, February 12, 2024

BILL Whitaker has been on the air as a television journalist for over 40 years but little is known about his personal life.

Whitaker has worked hard to keep his private life private.

Is Bill Whitaker married?

Whitaker is married to his wife Terry with who he shares two children, a son Gail and a daughter Lesley.

The couple is very private and little is known about their dating history.

The 60 minutes host moved to New York City with his family when he got the gig.

He has kept his personal life out the spotlight and his children out of the public eye.

What is Bill Whitaker's net worth?

Whitaker has a net worth of $1 million according to Featured Biography.

His annual salary at CBS is estimated to be $102,548.

He has earned his wealth with over 40 years in television journalism.

He is best known as a correspondent for his work on the CBS News program 60 Minutes.

Whitaker has been a correspondent in San Francisco, Charlotte, Atlanta, Los Angeles, and New York City.

When is Bill Whitaker hosting Jeopardy?

The popular game show returned on January 4, 2021, to challenge contestants' trivia knowledge.

Whitaker will take center stage as guest host of Jeopardy! after CNN's Anderson Cooper.

He will host the iconic show from May 3 to May 14.

Whitaker admitted that, “Jeopardy!' is my wife's and my favorite show” and that she encouraged him to accept the gig.

Actress Mayim Bialik will host the show after Whitaker from May 31- June 11.

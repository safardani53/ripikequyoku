Deshaun Watson Girlfriend Jilly Anais Has Her Own Cookbooks

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, February 10, 2024

Deshaun Watson and his girlfriend, Jilly Anais, have been in a strong and steady relationship ever since they first crossed paths at Catch restaurant in Los Angeles back in 2019.

Jilly Anais, the quarterback’s girlfriend, is a multi-talented individual, excelling as a model, social media influencer, and YouTube personality.

Additionally, she is an accomplished singer and actress with a diverse background.

Born in Houston, Texas, she made the move to Los Angeles at 18 to pursue her dreams in the entertainment industry.

Derrick Deshaun Watson, born on September 14, 1995, is an NFL quarterback who currently plays for the Cleveland Browns.

He attended Clemson University for college football, and during his time there, he played a key role in leading the team to a national championship victory in 2016.

Following his successful college career, he was drafted in the first round of the 2017 NFL Draft by the Houston Texans.

On March 18, 2022, a significant trade occurred, and the NFL star was transferred to the Cleveland Browns.

Subsequently, he inked a groundbreaking contract with the team, comprising a fully guaranteed five-year deal worth a staggering $230 million.

This landmark agreement is one of the largest contracts in NFL history and includes an unprecedented amount of guaranteed money.

Deshaun Watson Girlfriend, Jilly Anais

Deshaun Watson is happily dating his longtime girlfriend, Jilly Anais, who is her biggest supporter. 

Jilly, who is also an actress, began her acting journey with a debut in a 2013 episode of ‘East Los High,’ and she continued to showcase her talent, earning recognition for her role in the drama ‘God Send’ in 2019.

As a singer, Jilly has made her mark with several successful tracks, including “Sexy,” “Wait For It,” and “Milano,” with seven charting singles on iTunes.

Her immense talent has led to prominent appearances on American networks like the Grammy’s, NBC, ABC, and BET.

In 2022, she released an EP titled ‘COPY&Paste,’ solidifying her position as a rising star in the R&B / Pop genre.

With a massive online following, Jilly Anais boasts more than 2.4 million followers on Instagram and over 544k subscribers on YouTube.

Her journey to success started early, as she earned the title of Miss Teen Houston in 2011, showcasing her versatility as a former competitive cheerleader as well.

Clearly, Jilly Anais is only just beginning to build her Entertainment Empire, and her future in the industry looks incredibly promising.

Jilly Anais Has Her Own Cookbooks

The multi-talented lady is not only an expert in music and social media but also an exceptional chef. 

Frequently, Jilly enjoys hosting dinner parties with her friends, and her superstar boyfriend often joins in on the gatherings.

These occasions serve as opportunities for her to fine-tune her recipes and select the ones worthy of being included in her cookbooks.

Notably, she has already released two cookbooks: the first titled “This Boss Babe Can Cookbook,” which made its debut in 2020.

The second volume of her series, “She Cooks Too!,” was launched in 2022. Both books can be purchased through Amazon and her personal website.

Jilly Anais Supported Deshaun Watson Throughout His Scandal

In 2021, Derrick Deshaun Watson faced legal trouble when over two dozen female massage therapists filed lawsuits against him, alleging sexual harassment and assault.

Watson maintained his innocence and denied any wrongdoing, and has already settled 23 of these claims related to sexual misconduct. 

As a consequence of these serious accusations, on August 18, 2022, the NFL took action by suspending Watson for 11 games.

Additionally, he was ordered to pay a record-breaking fine of $5 million.

The league mandated that he undergo evaluation by behavioral experts and participate in a treatment program.

Notably, the $5 million fine, along with an additional $2 million, will be directed toward agencies focused on preventing sexual assault.

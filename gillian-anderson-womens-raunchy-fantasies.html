Sex Education star Gillian Anderson swamped with fans' raunchy fantasies on Instagram

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, September 7, 2024

SEX Education star Gillian Anderson has been swamped with thousands of raunchy fantasies from women all over the world for her new book.

She invited her 2.5million Instagram followers to share their most secret X-rated thoughts — telling them to “let your imagination run wild”.

Gillian, 54, promised that her own fantasies would be “right there beside yours.”

Submissions are now closed. Gillian — who plays sex therapist Dr Jean Milburn on the hit Netflix show — said: “We’ve been truly amazed by the response — from Nepal to the Netherlands, Bangladesh to Brazil, Algeria to America.

“This book has the potential to be a generation-defining exploration of women and sex.

"Thank you for trusting me with your deepest desires.”

The book, published by Bloomsbury, will be titled Dear Gillian.

As part of its launch, the ­former X Files star will donate £500 to charity for every published letter — split equally between War Child and Women for Women International.

Bloomsbury is also to donate £50,000 to Women for Women International.

Bella Hadid Biography, Age, Height, Boyfriend, Net Worth

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, February 3, 2024

Bella Hadid (born on 9 October 1996) is an American model. She entered the industry in 2012 and is associated with the renowned agency, IMG Models.

Known originally as Isabella Khairiah Hadid, she earned the title of “Model of the Year” in 2016 according to Models.com.

Follow us further to know her age, height, net worth, and boyfriend.

Page Contents

Who is Bella Hadid?

Bella Hadid is an American model. She has been in the industry since the year 2012 and works with the agency IMG Models.

In 2016, she was voted “Model of the Year” by industry professionals for Models.com. Isabella Khairiah Hadid is her name given at birth.

Over the span of four years, Hadid has made twenty-seven appearances on international Vogue magazine covers.

She has already built a great career in the modeling industry has is all the way up to the stars.

Early Life of Bella Hadid

Bella Hadid was born on October 9, 1996, in Washington, D.C. Her father, Mohamed Hadid, is a Palestinian Jordanian real estate developer, while her mother, Yolanda Hadid, is a Dutch former model.

Bella Hadid is an American by nationality and has her belief in the Islam religion.

Through her father, Hadid claims descent from Daher Al Omer, Prince of Nazareth and Sheik of Galilee. She has an older sister, Gigi, and a younger brother, Anwar, both of whom are also models.

She also has two older paternal half-sisters; Marielle and Alana. Hadid and her siblings were raised on a ranch in Santa Barbara, California.

The family moved to Beverly Hills after ten years.

As a teenager, Hadid was an equestrian and dreamed of competing at the 2016 Summer Olympics, although she competed in equitation, which is not an Olympic discipline.

She was diagnosed, along with her mother and brother, with chronic Lyme disease in 2012.

After graduating from Malibu High School in 2014, Hadid moved to New York City to study photography at the Parsons School of Design.

She suspended her studies to focus on her modeling career but has expressed an interest in returning to school once she’s done modeling to branch out into fashion photography.

Hadid has also expressed an interest in acting.

Bella Hadid Age

Bella Hadid is currently 27 years old as of 2023, having been born on October 9, 1996.

Despite her young age, she has already left a significant mark in the modeling industry since her debut in 2012 with IMG Models.

Bella Hadid Career

Hadid’s modeling career began when she was sixteen years old with a commercial project for Flynn Skye.

She also appeared in modeling projects such as Lesa Amoore’s “The Swan Settings”, alongside actor Ben Barnes, and Holly Copeland’s “Smoking Hot”.

Hadid also modelled for Hanna Hayes’ Fall/Winter 2013 Collection, and did campaign work for Chrome Hearts, the family brand of her best friend, Jesse Jo Stark.

After signing to IMG Models on August 21, 2014, she made her New York Fashion Week debut in September, walking for Desigual. In December, Hadid made her first cover appearance as a model in Jalouse Magazine.

In 2015, she made her official runway debut at Tom Ford’s Fall/Winter 2015 collection. In May, she walked for amfAR’s 22nd Cinema Against AIDS Gala.

In late 2015, she walked in the New York Fashion Week.

Hadid also modeled for Balmain at the Paris Fashion Week. She made her Chanel debut in December 2015.

She walked in the luxury brand’s Métiers d’Art show in Rome for the first time. She made her first cover appearance on ‘Jalouse Magazine’ in December 2014.

In 2017, Hadid broke the record for her appearance in five Vogue September covers in one year. These included international editions of the magazine- China, Spain, Brazil, Australia and Arabia.

The popular model has won multiple awards, including the Model of the Year at the Daily Front Row’s Fashion Los Angeles Awards and the Model of the Year at the GQ Men of the Year Awards, both in 2016.

Bella Hadid Boyfriend

Bella Hadid is currently in a relationship with Adan Banuelos. Before him, she was dating her ex-boyfriend Marc Kalman.

Before Marc, she also dated Matthew Morton, Stella Maxwell, Odell Beckham Jr, Anthony Joshua, and Jordan Clarkson.

She is also vocal about political issues around the Israel-Palestine conflict, protesting against former US President Trump’s move to shift the American Embassy to Jerusalem from Tel Aviv.

Hadid has supported the Black Lives Matter movement. In May 2020, Hadid announced that she would be donating to the NAACP Legal Defense Fund.

In July, Hadid auctioned off self-made clothing designs, with all proceeds going to organizations like Your Rights Camp and Feeding America.

Underage drinking and arrest

However, she has also faced legal issues. In July 2014, she was arrested and charged with a DUI for underage drinking.

Her driver’s license was suspended for a year and she was given six months of probation.

Hadid was also ordered to perform 25 hours of mandatory community service and attend 20 hours of meetings in the Alcoholics Anonymous organization.

Famous sister Gigi Hadid

Gigi Hadid who is an American model, television personality, founder, creator, and director of the clothing line “Guest In Residence” is her sister.

In November 2014, she made her debut in the Top 50 Models ranking at Models.com. In 2016, she was named International Model of the Year by the British Fashion Council.

Over four years, she has made thirty-five appearances on international Vogue magazine covers.

She has appeared in numerous high fashion campaigns, and editorials and is a spokesmodel for Maybelline.

Who are Hugh Hefners children and where are they now?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, March 30, 2024

PLAYBOY guru Hugh Hefner was notoriously known for wild parties with his famous"bunnies."

When he was not running his media empire, he was often found at home spending time with his kids.

Who are Hugh Hefner's kids?

Over the years, the late Hugh Hefner welcomed four children from his previous marriages.

His children include:

Christie Hefner

Born November 8, 1952, Christie, 69, is known today as an American businesswoman and activist.

Christie is Hugh's eldest child from his marriage to Mildred Williams.

She graduated with a bachelor's degree in English and American literature from Brandeis University in 1974.

In 1982, she became president of Playboy Enterprises and was made chairman of the board and CEO in 1988.

She stepped down from her position as CEO in 2009 and has kept a low profile ever since.

David Hefner

Born August 30, 1955, David, 66, is famously known as Hugh's son.

He attended the University of California Santa Cruz majoring in film and digital media and later launched his own computer consulting firm.

He has also dabbled in film production.

David's first independent film, Forgotten Pills, won the Grand Jury prize for best feature at the 2010 Dances with Films festival.

His second feature Washed was released in 2014.

Despite the famous Hefner name, David has stayed out of the spotlight for most of his life.

Marston Hefner

Born April 9, 1990, Marston, 31, is famously known as the first son of Hugh and former "Playmate of the Year" Kimberly Conrad.

During the first years of his life, he lived at the Playboy mansion.

In 2012, Marston was arrested after he allegedly attacked his Playmate of the Year girlfriend Claire Sinclair, according to TMZ.

Marston has also not followed in his father's footsteps and instead has launched a career as a writer.

His Twitter bio states "writer and backgammon" and he says he has written a zombie book.

Cooper Hefner

Born September 4, 1991, Cooper, 30, is known today as an American businessman, teacher, author, activist, and reservist in the United States Air Force.

Cooper graduated with a bachelor's degree from Chapman University in 2015 and started working at Playboy Enterprises while he was in college.

In 2016 he launched the media startup company, Hop.

In July of the same year, he returned to Playboy as a chief creative officer when his father stepped down.

In December 2019, Cooper then enlisted in the United States Air Force and married Harry Potter actress Scarlett Byrne.

The couple announced in November 2021 that they are expecting twins and "couldn't be happier."

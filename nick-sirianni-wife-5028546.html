Facts About Eagles Coachs Spouse Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 9, 2024
  • Nick Sirianni is the coach of the Philadelphia Eagles, who are playing in the 2023 Super Bowl.
  • He is married to Brett Ashley Cantwell, who he married in 2013.
  • Nick and Brett have three children together.

Nick Sirianni is coaching in the 2023 Super Bowl, and his wife, Brett Ashley Cantwell, will be there cheering him on. Nick is the coach of the Philadelphia Eagles, who are taking on the Kansas City Chiefs in the big game on Feb. 12. Interestingly, Nick used to serve as an assistant coach for the Kansas City Chiefs, so he’ll be going up against his former team.

Nick has been coaching in the NFL since 2009, and Brett has been his biggest supporter since they’ve gotten together. She was seen on the field sending some luck to her hubby before the Super Bowl started, and will be watching the game from a suite. Learn more about Brett with five facts:

How Did Nick & Brett Meet?

Nick and Brett met in 2011 when he was working for the Kansas City Chiefs. He started working for the team in 2009 as the offensive quality control coach, and was eventually promoted to wide receiver coach. He was with the team until 2013. Meanwhile, Brett is a Missouri native, and the two were living in the same apartment complex when Nick was working there. Brett’s sister eventually convinced her to go out with Nick.

“Obviously, meeting my wife there, that will always be a special place for us,” Nick told The Athletic. “Then professionally, my first stint in the NFL. Obviously it was really important both ways.” Nick and Brett went on to tie the knot in June 2013, just two years after meeting.

Nick Sirianni & Brett Have 3 Children

In the ten years since they got married, Nick and Brett went on to have three children together: two sons, Miles and Jacob, and a daughter, Taylor, who is 5. Brett and Nick mostly keep their children out of the public eye. However, when the Eagles won the NFC Championship in January, the kids joined their dad at a press conference post-game.

What Does Nick Sirianni’s Wife Do?

These days, Brett is a supportive wife and mom amidst her husband’s busy career. However, when she first met Nick, she was working as a teacher in Missouri. Brett ended her teaching career in 2013, just weeks before she married Nick. “I loved my job, I loved everything about Kansas City,” she admitted. “I loved Nick more, so it was all worth it.”

Where Does Brett Live?

After marrying Nick, Brett moved several times for the sake of her husband’s career. The two have lived in San Diego, Orange County, California and Indianapolis. Now, the family lives in New Jersey, which is where they’ve resided since Nick started coaching the Eagles in 2021.

‘I was from Missouri and had never moved out of Missouri,” Brett admitted, of moving to San Diego. “I hit reality and realized I’m in the most beautiful place ever, but it’s kind of difficult because he works all the time.”

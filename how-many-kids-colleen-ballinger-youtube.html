How many kids does Colleen Ballinger have?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, May 9, 2024

YOUTUBE sensation Colleen Ballinger has over 8.5million subscribers on the platform where she often posts vlogs about her life.

Fans of the 36-year-old comedian want to know more about her marriage to Erik Stocklin and their children.

How many kids does Colleen Ballinger have?

Colleen Ballinger has a total of three children, whom she shares with her second husband, Erik Stocklin.

The couple's first child was born on December 11, 2018. Their son's name is Flynn Timothy.

On November 6, 2021, Ballinger gave birth to twins - a boy and a girl.

She documented the birth of Wesley and Maisy on her YouTube channel and revealed that the babies had arrived in an emergency C-section due to a "dangerous umbilical cord complication called cord prolapse."

Ballinger was two months along when she announced her pregnancy in June, adding that she was due in December 2021.

After a visit to the doctor in May, Ballinger expressed her joy about the pregnancy, saying: “I'm just so happy. I wanted this so bad. We're so excited. We really were just trying and hoping for this. This is very exciting.

"That's a very shocking thing to say because, if you've watched me at all in the last three years, all I ever say is I'm never getting pregnant again."

She added: “That was the plan until I got pregnant."

What did Colleen Ballinger say about her pregnancy?

In a May 2021 YouTube video, Ballinger recounted being on a family vacation and experiencing “concerning complications” while there.

She discussed having a nightmare prior to finding out she was pregnant, saying: “The last morning [of vacation] I woke up from a nightmare that I was having a miscarriage, which really freaked me out because I've never dreamed about a miscarriage before. 

“I was on edge from the second I woke up. And then I started bleeding and it looked exactly like it did when I had my miscarriage."

"It really freaked me out," she admitted. "I was confident that it was happening again."

According to her doctor, Ballinger found out she had a polyp that her body was trying to get rid of.

"We do an ultrasound and she goes, 'You're not having a miscarriage. You're having twins,'" Ballinger recalled.

"In a matter of 10 seconds I went from I'm having another loss … to I'm having twins," she said.

The actress also revealed that she is having di/di twins, which is known as a dichorionic diamniotic pregnancy.

Essentially, the twins each have their own chorionic and amniotic sacs and Ballinger will not know if they are fraternal or identical until they are born.

Who is Colleen Ballinger's husband?

Colleen is married to Stocklin, an actor from New Jersey.

He is known for his recurring roles on television series such as Mistresses, Stalker, and Good Trouble, and for his leading role in the Netflix original series Haters Back Off.

In 2021, Stocklin and Ballinger began a weekly podcast titled Relax! with Colleen and Erik.

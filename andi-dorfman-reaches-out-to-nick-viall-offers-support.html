Andi Dorfman Reaches Out to Nick Viall, Offers Support

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, March 1, 2024

The Bachelorette star Nick Viall isn’t having the best week of his life, to put it mildly. But he is getting support from a surprising source!

After Monday’s finale of The Bachelorette, he was dumped by Kaitlyn Bristowe, who got engaged to Shawn Booth instead.

Viall was shocked by Kaitlyn’s choice, but Andi Dorfman, last season’s Bachelorette who dumped Viall for Josh Murray, came to his rescue.

Dorfman and Viall haven’t been on good terms since Viall revealed they had sex in the fantasy suite.  Loose lips sink ships, buddy.

However, Andi still had kind words for Nick after Kaitlyn broke his heart and sent him packing.

“As much as I don’t care for Nick, I don’t wish a broken heart on anyone,” she tweeted. 

Dorfman, 28, offered Nick some advise on what to do with the pricey Neil Lane diamond engagement he had a planned on giving to Kaitlyn.

“If I were Nick I would have swallowed that 80k ring and said oops I lost it…” she said on Twitter.

This advice is kinder than the advice Andi gave Kaitlyn when she found out Viall would be on the show: “One word…. RUN! Okay two… RUN FAST!”

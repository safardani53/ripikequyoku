AGT fans fear for Sofia Vergara's marriage to Joe Manganiello as they spot 'worrying' pattern in new

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, April 28, 2024

SOFIA Vergara's fans have shared their concerns that her marriage to Joe Manganiello could be in trouble.

The America's Got Talent judge has been celebrating her 51st birthday in Italy with two of her friends - but the True Blood actor, 46, has been noticeably absent.

Sofia shared several photos and videos from her birthday dinner at a restaurant on Monday evening.

In them, the Modern Family actress grinned as she blew out the candles on her cake and was serenaded by a band.

Posing with her friends, she wrote: "So lucky to have my best friends in my favorite restaurant in my favorite island!!! I love u."

Fans flooded the comments with their concern after noticing that Joe was missing from the night's celebrations and hadn't joined her on the Italian getaway.

One wrote: "But where is hubby? Not in a picture anywhere. And they put their house up for sale."

Last month, Sofia and Joe put their $18million Beverly Hills mansion up for sale for the second time in a year.

Sofia - who is best known for playing Gloria on Modern Family - bought the seven-bedroom house in 2014 for $10.6million before she married Joe.

Another fan asked "Where’s Joe? I’m worried," and a third speculated "Definitely trouble."

They also pointed out that Sofia - who married Joe in 2015 - appeared to not be wearing her wedding ring in the snaps.

Others pointed out Joe's birthday tribute to Sofia on his feed and claimed something felt off.

The actor shared a low resolution photo of him and Sofia together on Instagram and simply captioned it: "Feliz Cumpleaños Sofía!!!"

One user commented: "Joe, I hope you guys are ok, that was a very different(cold) happy birthday wish to your wife, compared to previous ones!"

Another added: "Am I the only one who doesn’t like the way it sounds? 'Sofia'.... not even 'My love, My beloved wife.'"

A third follower posted: "Something is off.

"Cold message for her and has two stories about his dog’s birthday. And Sofia has not even said thank you."

Joe was also missing from Sofia's Fourth of July celebrations earlier this month.

The actress enjoyed a pool party with her son Manolo, 31, as well as her rarely-seen sister Sandra and other family members.

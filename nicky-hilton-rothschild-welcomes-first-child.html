Nicky Hilton Rothschild Welcomes First Child!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, April 14, 2024

Nicky Hilton Rothschild is officially a mother!

The heiress welcomed her first child into the world on July 8. 

She and her husband, James Rothschild already have a name for their little bundle of joy. 

They called her Lily Grace Victoria Rothschild. 

It’s such a beautiful name. Right?!

News of the pregnancy first came out in January. 

Nicky married James in a lavish wedding in London last July, so their new addition to the family came around one-year after said wedding. 

Nicky opted to go for two baby showers and we can only imagine how lavish they would have been. 

Paris Hilton’s sister has kept a relatively low profile over the last few years, but she sure seems happy. 

However, her life still isn’t without drama.

We congratulate the couple on the birth of their child.

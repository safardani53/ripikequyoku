Lady Gaga spills out of tiny nude top and bares her cleavage in new sultry snap

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

LADY Gaga sizzled in a skintight nude top as she flashed her cleavage for a flirty shot.

The Poker Face singer, 35, struck a playful pose flicking back her hair as she filmed herself lying on her stomach in her garden.

The cheeky camera angle captured a glimpse of her chest as she went bra-free in the low-cut garment, wearing its straps on the sides of her shoulders.

The A Star Is Born actress left her dark blonde hair falling in a straight style and, while only her mouth was in shot, she appeared to go make-up free.

Gaga uploaded the super short clip with a series of colorful Emoji love hearts while her fans were quick to comment underneath.

One gushed: "OKAY UR HOT," as another posted: "OMG GAGAAA."

A third continued: "We love you Gaga."

One then surmized: "Such a beautiful woman! X."

The pop queen recently stripped to a tiny thong bikini ahead of her stint on the Friends reunion show.

In the long-awaited episode, Gaga joined Lisa Kudrow, 57, to perform her character Phoebe Buffay's song Smelly Cat.

Director Ben Winston, 39, recently confirmed the duet between the two actresses and explained how the idea came about. 

Ben said: "I said to Lisa, 'Would you be prepared to sing it?' and she was like, 'Yeah, that would be fun.'"

"We went through a few names and we both agreed that Gaga, if we could get her, would be the ultimate one because she associates with and feels close to Phoebe in so many ways, as Gaga says on the show.

"That was a really beautiful moment, and Lady Gaga jumped at the chance to do it."

Buzz Cut Hairstyle Would You Shave Your Head Like Katy Perry? Hollywood Life

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, July 25, 2024

The buzz cut is turning into THE style of the season! Do you think you could rock a shaved head like Kristen Stewart or Cara Delevingne? Before you shave your head, read these tips from famous hairstylist Rodney Cutler of Cutler Salons. He says it’s not as easy as just using a razor at home. It’s definitely a salon job! “Hand-crafting the shape first with scissors will allow you to adapt the shape of the cut to the face before using clippers,” Rodney says. You don’t need to go full-on shaved head — you could also rock a super short pixie like Zoe Kravitz.

A hairstyle like this is a lot of work — Rodney says you need a trim every three to five weeks. He also says that anyone can rock it, despite head or face shape. “It has more to do with wanting to buzz your hair and that confidence, than ideal face or head shapes,” he says.

HollywoodLifers, would you ever shave your head? Do you like this buzz cut hairstyle?

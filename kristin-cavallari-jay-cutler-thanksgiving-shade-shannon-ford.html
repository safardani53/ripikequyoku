Kristin Cavallari shades her ex-husband Jay Cutler after he spends Thanksgiving with Carrie Underwoo

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 19, 2024

KRISTIN Cavallari appeared to throw some shade at ex-husband Jay Cutler after he spent Thanksgiving with pal Carrie Underwood and Shannon Ford.

The 33-year-old shared a photo of a quote that said: “Happiness is an inside job. Don’t assign anyone else that much power over your life.”

Above it, with the twinkling Christmas lights in the background, Kristin had written “Truth.” 

Fans will be speculating that mom of three was throwing shade at her ex-husband, who spent his Thanksgiving holiday in Charleston, while Kristin and their children were over 500 miles away in Nashville. 

Jay, 37, posted a video of the turkey to his Instagram account, joking that there was enough food for 20 people. 

Then, appearing to stir the pot some more, Jay shared a clip of him enjoying wine and oysters with Shannon. 

Fans of his ex-wife's reality series, Very Cavallari, will remember Shannon, 27, had been a fixture in the early days of the show until Kristin fired her.

The former quarterback posted the video of himself with Ford and Mark 'Chuy' Block on Saturday after Thanksgiving.

Clearly thinking the same thing, many fans wrote underneath to comment on the reunion. 

One wrote: “Stir that pot Jay. Stir that pot.”

“Petty Jay is the best Jay,” added another along with a teapot emoji. 

While a third penned: “THIS IS DRAMA”

In the short video Jay looked relaxed in a patterned button-up shirt while sporting bed head hair.

His pal Shannon looked stylish in a black and white check shirt and white wide-brimmed hat. 

Kristin, who owns jewelry brand Uncommon James, fired Shannon on the season two premiere of the reality series. 

According to Kristin, Shannon had been missing meetings, not showing up to the office, ignoring deadlines, and being outright rude to the boss herself.

After the sacking, Shannon posted a series of shade-worth Instagram photos leading many to speculate that the two’s once firm friendship was well and truly over. 

The meet up is sure to rile Kristen, who announced her split from Jay in April, with the former Laguna Beach star admitting it was the "hardest decision" she's ever made.

The Uncommon James founder told People: "I always thought it was interesting when people would refer to us as 'couple goals.'"

Kristin explained her relationship breakdown "didn't happen overnight."

She revealed: “We tried really, really hard for years and years. It was the hardest decision I've ever made."

The reality star opened up about also walking away from her own series Very Cavallari and how she managed to keep her marital woes off the screen.

“She said: We definitely kept a lot of stuff private.

“Producers saw stuff, but they didn't put it in the show — which is good, because I don't want my kids to ever [see that].”

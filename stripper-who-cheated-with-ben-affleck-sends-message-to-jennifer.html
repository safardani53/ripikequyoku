Stripper Who Cheated With Ben Affleck Sends Message to Jennifer Garner: RUN!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Lately, it seems like each new day brings new indications that it’s over between Ben Affleck and Jennifer Garner.

But while most of the bad omens have revolved around new revelations of Ben’s cheating, today’s is a blast from the past comes from the days of Bennifer 1.0.

Some of you may not even remember the days when Ben and Jennifer Lopez but were an item, but we’re to tell you:

The early 2000s were a strange time, and it totally happened.

Not only has Ben repeated his pattern by getting involved with another Jen, but he’s once again spending a lot of time in Vegas and allegedly sleeping around.

As 41-year-old exotic dancer and former Ben-banger Tammy Morris reminds, us none of that is new. 

Tammy spoke with Radar Online about sleeping with Affleck while he was still dating Lopez, and she has one message for Ben’s long-suffering wife: GET OUT WHILE YOU CAN!!!

“I believe, ‘Once a cheater always a cheater.’ My advice to Jen is: ‘Run!’” Morrison says. 

Though she gushes about her time with Ben as “a night [she’ll] never forget,” Morrison says she believes Affleck is womanizer to the core, who will never be husband material. 

Thanks for the advice, Tammy, but we’re sure Jen has figured that out on her own by now.

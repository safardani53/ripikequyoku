Im a size 16 and Ive found the best bodysuit everit sucks me in, I don't have to wear a bra

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 7, 2024

A PLUS-SIZE fashion fan has taken to social media to show off her brand new bodysuit, which left her very impressed. 

Elisabeth Carley Leonard is a size 16 fashionista from Leicester, who recently shared a post to TikTok, showing off her £10 bargain buy.

Elisabeth managed to get her hands on a stunning brown bodysuit, which she described as a dupe for Skims shapewear.

But don’t worry, if you can’t afford to splash the cash on the pricey Kim Kardashian bodysuits, you’ll want to check out this cheap buy.  

Elisabeth shared her clip on the video sharing platform with the caption ‘This bodysuit I am OBSESSED. I got the size XL in chocolate brown and love pairing it with jeans and a blazer for a simple but chic look.” 

In the clip, we saw Elisabeth try on her new bodysuit, which she described as “the best bodysuit EVER.”

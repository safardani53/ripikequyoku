Naomi Smalls’ Real Name

Smalls’s real name is Davis Heppenstall.

He was a runner-up in season 8 of RuPaul’s Drag Race in 2016.

Now, he’s returning for All Stars season 4!

The name Naomi Smalls is a combination of supermodel Naomi Campbell and rap legend The Notorious B.I.G (AKA Biggie Smalls) – therefore giving her the recognisable name she has today.

Naomi Smalls Instagram

Naomi has a whopping one million followers on Instagram.

As well as that she has 99.3k Twitter followers.

In February 2018 Noami interviewed rapper Cardi B for Cosmopolitan magazine.

How Old is Naomi Smalls?

Noami was born September 8th 1993.

This makes her 25 years old and a Virgo.

She was born in Redlands, California and has 11 brothers and sisters!

In an interview with Fader magazine, Naomi said: “I’m very thankful I get to live my dream. I never thought that when I was watching Drag Race as a 15-year-old junior in high school that I would be able to travel the world and work with these legends.”

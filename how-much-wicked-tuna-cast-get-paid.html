How much do the Wicked Tuna cast get paid?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 8, 2024

NATIONAL Geographic’s series Wicked Tuna follows the life of several Massachusetts fishermen as they hunt for tuna in the Atlantic Ocean.

The show is one of the most popular in the network, spanning over 10 seasons since its premiere in 2012.

How much do the Wicked Tuna cast get paid?

The Wicked Tuna cast make a hefty sum of money, reported to be $100,000 per episode.

Their salary increased drastically as the popularity of the show did too.

During the beginning stages of the series, cast members would make approximately $2,000 - $3,000 per episode, not including the fish they would catch.

Bluefin tuna are one of the most expensive fish and can average about $40 per pound. 

A single fish can cost up to $20,000.

When does Wicked Tuna premiere?

Season 10 of Wicked Tuna premiers on March 5 at 9pm EST on National Geographic.

The show airs every Sunday on the network.

Following the season debut episode, National Geographic put together a compilation of clips of Duffy and Tyler fishing together to honor his memory.

What happened to Duffy on Wicked Tuna?

Duffy, aka Nicholas Fudge, passed away before the crew started filming season eight of the series.

His cause of death was not announced at the time of his passing almost three years ago, but fans were shocked to learn that he died so suddenly.

His colleagues inferred that may have succumbed to ‘the bends’ or decompression sickness.

Decompression sickness, also called generalized barotrauma, "refers to injuries caused by a rapid decrease in the pressure that surrounds you, of either air or water," according to Harvard Health.

Although it most commonly affects deep-sea divers and scuba divers, it also can occur during high-altitude or unpressurized air travel.

What is the Wicked Tuna spin-off series?

After season two of Wicked Tuna, National Geographic released a spin-off set off in the outerbanks of North Carolina.

The show was originally named Wicked Tuna: North vs. South but was renamed Wicked Tuna: Outer Banks in its second season.

The spin-off is on its seventh season, with the latest episode airing in October 2020.

The series is available to stream on Disney+.

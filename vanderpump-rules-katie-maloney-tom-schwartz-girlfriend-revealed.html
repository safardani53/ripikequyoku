Vanderpump Rules stars Katie Maloney and Tom Schwartz shared girlfriend revealed as bisexual s

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, February 18, 2024

VANDERPUMP Rules fans are talking about only one girl after seeing the explosive new trailer for Season 11 - and for once it isn't Rachel Leviss.

There are many shocking twists in the upcoming episodes, which will air in January, including a love triangle featuring Katie Maloney and her ex-husband Tom Schwartz.

Viewers will see them both lock lips with a new pink-haired cast member, who has been revealed as singer-songwriter Tori Keeth.

Tom admits he's never been in a love triangle before, while Katie declares: "May the best one win," in the new teaser.

And although it may seem 25-year-old Tori has come out of nowhere, The U.S. Sun can exclusively reveal she has had a front-row seat to last season's drama and is well-known among the cast.

Not only has Tori been a long-time friend of Scheana Shay for years, but she also performed at the mom of one's wedding to Australian rugby player Brock Davies.

Unearthed pictures posted to X, formerly Twitter, show her at the Dreams Natura Resort & Spa in Cancún, Mexico, posing by the pool.

Fans of the show will recognize the luxurious spot as the same place Schwartz kissed Rachel Leviss, who used to go by Raquel, which sent his ex Katie into a tailspin.

Old social media comments also show Tori had Scheana's back when she was accused of punching Rachel and giving her a black eye, which she denied.

Scheana, who was furious Rachel hooked up with Tom Sandoval behind his then-girlfriend Ariana Madix's back, was never charged with a crime.

Rachel later dropped her restraining order request.

DEFENDING SCHEANA

Tori shared on social media at the time: "What you’ve done is disgusting enough. But to LIE about a black eye? Come on Raquel, we’re not stupid. You are. You've had this black eye since BEFORE you were 'supposedly' punched."

She further blasted her for trying to "f**k with Scheana's life" by making up the claims, which she dubbed "sad attempts to take the heat off of [her] despicable actions."

Having previously hung out with Rachel, she later added: "Drop the victim card raquel. We all see right through you. Damn you really think you know somebody."

The new Bravo cast member has also previously appeared on Scheana's podcast, and even admitted to kissing her pal on a wild night out in a ploy to get someone else's attention.

According to archive comments, she has also spent time with former Pump Rules star Kristen Doute who has supported her singing career.

Tori, originally from Seattle, is no stranger to being in front of the camera and will likely take to reality TV like a duck to water.

She has been acting since a young age and has plenty of credits on her IMDB page, having starred in Henry Danger, The Message, and American-Born Chinese.

The rising star will help build her growing fan base by appearing on the show weeks after dropping the music video for her track One More Night.

DATING HISTORY

She has already amassed 108,000 followers on Instagram and released her album Lovesick in 2022 which has been popular on streaming services.

Tori revealed in a tweet in 2020 that she identifies as bisexual and often tweets about wanting a girlfriend, but is believed to currently be single.

She previously dated fellow artist Kyson Facer in 2018 and Matt Soto a year later, before they broke up in 2020.

Katie, 36, and her ex Schwartz, 41, have seen their relationship drama play out on Vanderpump Rules in recent years and announced their split in March 2022 after 12 years together.

They finalized their divorce in October last year and have only been casually dating other people since.

Returning stars included LaLa Kent, 33; Scheana Shay, 38; James Kennedy, 31; Tom Schwartz, 41; Katie Maloney, 36; Ariana Madix, 38; and Tom Sandoval, 40.

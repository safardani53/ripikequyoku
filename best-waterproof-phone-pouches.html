12 Best Waterproof Phone Pouches | The Sun 2021

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, October 1, 2024

SUMMER is on the horizon, and a waterproof phone pouch should be one of your top priorities if you want to keep your phone in tip-top shape.

But with even the average smartphone setting you back hundreds you'll want to make sure it's protected when you're off on your summer holidays, spending a day by the pool, or at the waterpark.

We can all admit that our phones have become an extension of us, in our hand or back pocket at any given moment, even in conditions that aren't exactly meant for our digital devices.

So despite most phones coming with a level of water protection, you don't want to end up with it in a bag of rice hoping it will switch back on for the sake of an underwater selfie, instead invest in a waterproof pouch.

We have rounded up all the best choices on the market for all budgets, so whether you are swimming, cycling, hiking or just going to the beach, we've got you covered.

How much does a waterproof phone pouch cost?

The market is full of choices when it comes to waterproof phone pouches, so you can splash out or opt for a bargain depending on your budget, but generally, you can expect to spend between £10-£30.

That being said we've spotted some high-end options like the Lifeproof FRĒ which can set you back almost £70, or on the other end of the scale, the Drybag which will cost you just £6.50, so it really comes down to what features you are expecting to get for your money.

Are dry bags waterproof?

As the name suggests, Drybags aren't completely waterproof, they are better described as water-resistant, they will keep your phone safe and dry from splashes and spills but aren't designed to be jumping in the pool with.

But they do come with a level of protection, for instance, the Drybag we've seen comes with an IP66 rating, meaning it can be briefly submerged in water and can float.

What is the best waterproof phone pouch?

When it comes to best we always say it depends on what you're looking for, if you want something basic to keep your device safe and the splashes and dust away then the Decathlon will suit you perfectly.

But if you want to be in the pool taking photos and your phone fully protected underwater then one of our top picks would be the OverBoard, which comes with an IP68 rating to certify it as 100% water proof.

Are waterproof phone pouches safe?

If bought from a reputable brand, waterproof pouches can be very safe - some are even able to float or last for hours after being submerged in metres of water.

Your best bet is to purchase a 100% waterproof phone pouch, with a double or triple lock and airtight seal.

They usually come with the option to clip the pouch to your belt or backpack, or even come with a lanyard to hang around your neck for added security.

Where to buy a waterproof phone pouch

Your best bet is to head online when it comes to buying a waterproof phone pouch.

We've found that sports or running high-street shops like Decathlon have plenty of options, and Amazon has a countless array of phone pouches in a variety of colours, sizes and price points.

How to clean a waterproof phone pouch

It's as easy as washing your hands! Simply open and submerge in warm, soapy water then pat dry with a paper towel — but be sure to double-check the brand's instructions prior to cleaning.

We would also recommend using a dust cloth after it is dry to remove any dust or lint before use.

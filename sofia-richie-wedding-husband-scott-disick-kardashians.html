Kardashian fans praise Scott Disicks ex Sofia Richie for escaping famous family as she marr

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

SOFIA Richie has been praised by fans for making her way out of the famous family by tying the knot.

The ex of Scott Disick and daughter of Lionel Richie married posh music executive Elliot Grainge on Saturday in France.

Sofia, 24, has found her forever in Elliot, 30, and they tied the knot in France on Saturday quite a lavish affair.

The model and British businessman said “I do” at Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc in Antibes in front of family and friends, including her sister Nicole Richie and friend Paris Hilton.

Lionel, 73, walked his daughter down the aisle at the serene outdoor wedding with 200 attendees.

For the ceremony, the bride wore a custom Chanel wedding dress, that featured an embellished train with white and iridescent beads, per Page Six.

It also had her “something blue” sewn in - an “S&E” with the date underneath.

Sofia invited Vogue Weddings to get a sneak peek at her Chanel dress before she walked down the aisle on their Instagram.

The outlet wrote: "A few weeks before the wedding, Sofia invited Vogue to an exclusive preview of her final fitting, where she gushed about the groom: 'I’m so excited to be married just because I’m marrying Elliot, I know that sounds so cheesy, but he’s the man of my dreams.'"

Under the post, however, fans congratulated Sofia not just on nuptials but also for exiting the inner sanctum of the Kardashian bubble for good.

'GOODBYE KARDASHIANS, HELLO TASTE!'

One Instagram user snarked: "Congratulations on getting rid of the Kardashians."

"Goodbye Kardashians and bad taste, hello sophistication and elegance! beautiful Sofia!" wrote another.

"She finally escaped from the Kardashians!" a user said.

"Poor Scott. Two for two," wrote a fan referring to his ex Kourtney Kardashian, 43.

IMPRESSIVE HUBBY

Elliot owns the independent record label, 10K Projects.

Based in Los Angeles, California, the company was founded in 2020.

According to the 10K Projects website, their purpose "is to recognize and champion the unique visions of the storytellers and creatives of tomorrow - as they see it, without compromise or regard for convention."

Elliot has overseen successful projects by Trippie Redd, Ice Spice, and Iann Dior.

He is the son of Sir Lucian Grainge, the chairman and CEO of Universal Music Group.

Sofia was first spotted with Elliot in January 2021 when rumors started to swirl that the pair were an item.

The couple went public with their relationship when she made it Instagram official on April 7, 2021.

'CALL ME GOOD LUCK CHUCK'

On April 20, 2022, the couple announced their engagement via Instagram.

Sofia posted a picture of her now-husband on one knee with the caption "forever isn't long enough."

Sofia and Elliot have known each other since they were children; both of their fathers are involved in the music industry.

Regarding their connection, an insider told ET: "Sofia and Elliot are happy together and having fun."

Sofia made headlines when she was spotted with Kourtney's ex in 2017.

Scott and Sofia dated until the summer of 2020, when they split for good.

Scott responded to ex-girlfriend Sofia's engagement news to Elliot with a ridiculous clap back.

Scott captioned a video from his boat: "In the 305 just call me good luck chuck," alluding to the 2007 Romantic Comedy of the same name.

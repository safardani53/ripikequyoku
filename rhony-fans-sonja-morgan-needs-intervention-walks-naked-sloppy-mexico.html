RHONY | The US Sun

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, April 20, 2024

RHONY fans are worried for Sonja Morgan and claim she “needs an intervention” after a “sloppy” night of drinking during a girl’s trip to Cancun, Mexico.

On night one of the beach getaway, the 56-year-old stripped completely naked as she walked around the oceanfront mansion that was full of staff.

As the Housewives were getting ready for a lesson on making ceviche, Sonja remained in bed with red wine.

Without letting go of the glass, she then screamed “blow dry girl, I’m coming in” and rolled off the mattress topless.

As she had a glam team get her ready, she claimed to costars Dorinda Medley, 55, and Luann de Lesseps, 55, that she had “not had anything” to drink that night.

However, Leah McSweeney, 37, and her other cast mates agree Sonja has been drinking.

Once ready, she popped out from the chair completely naked and showed her bare butt along with the rest of her body as she roamed uncovered.

Dorinda tries to get Sonja to sleep it off but she refuses and joins the group downstairs for dinner.

As they’re seated at the outdoor bar getting a private culinary class, Sonja slurs throughout the heated fight between rivals Leah and Ramona Singer.

The Sonja Morgan fashion designer orders “Coke with Rosé” - which Luann claimed she had been drinking previously in the night.

Bravo fans are concerned for the reality star’s behavior after tonight’s episode following a season of “heavy drinking” from Sonja.

One person tweeted: “I am so embarrassed for #Sonja. Season after season! Where's her bio family? Where's her Intervention. She needs help @BravoTV.”

“Sonja really needs an intervention, I'm sick of this display every other scene,” another said.

“How all these crazy b***hes sit there & ignore the fact that Sonja is in need of a serious intervention is beyond me,” a fan wrote.

While one Twitter user questioned: “Sonja just slurred ‘blow dry girl, i’m coming in’ while drunkenly rolling out bed topless and *leah’s* behavior is problematic?”

The worrying behavior came during a tense fight between Leah and Ramona - who previously told Sonja that her nemesis is "bipolar" and acts "uncontrollable" while drinking due to being on "medication."

After avoiding each other the whole trip, Leah was forced to confront her rival and said: "I really don't have that much to say for once in my life except that I'm really disappointed that you would talk about my depression issues behind my back as a way to belittle me when I've actually overcome all of them."

"Maybe I'm trying to make an excuse for your behavior, how you hurt me," Ramona fired back.

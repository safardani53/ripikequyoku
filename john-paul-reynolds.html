Who is John Paul Reynolds?

John Paul Reynolds is an American actor and writer. He is well-known for playing the lead in the television series Search Party (2016–2022) and appearing occasionally in Stranger Things (2016–2022).

In 2019, Reynolds also played a prominent role in the miniseries Four Weddings and a Funeral.

John and his coworkers won a Screen Actors Guild Award in 2017 for “Stranger Things” for their outstanding ensemble performance in a drama series.

He started his career at the Annoyance Theatre in Chicago after being born in Madison, Wisconsin.

Early Life of John Paul Reynolds

John Paul Reynolds was born on 5th August 1991 and is 31 years of age as of 2022. He was born in Madison, Wisconsin United States, and holds American nationality.

John’s parents, who he respects by maintaining their seclusion, raised him in Madison. He hasn’t mentioned any siblings, thus it’s assumed that he is an only child.

In his adolescent years, he developed an interest in acting, and his debut performance was in a play at the Annoyance Theatre in Chicago, Illinois.

John was among the top students in his high school as a result of his parents’ advice to concentrate on his studies rather than pursue acting.

He graduated from high school in 2009, and although he hasn’t discussed his subsequent schooling. It is widely assumed that he attended college and earned a Bachelor of Arts degree there in 2012.

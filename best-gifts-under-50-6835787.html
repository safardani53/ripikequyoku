The 58 Best Gifts Under $50 of 2024

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 29, 2024

The holiday season can bring good tidings and cheer, but it can also come with some financial stress. For those working with an extensive gift list, the presents can add up quickly, putting a dent in your budget. But that doesn’t mean you have to opt out of gifting this year.

By sticking with gifts that clock in under $50, you can ensure your holiday shopping gets done and family and friends are properly surprised — all within a budget you’re comfortable with. We considered PEOPLE editors' top picks, looked at celebrities' favorite gifts, and asked experts in their fields — beauty, travel, and more — to share their picks for the best gifts under $50.

Whether you’re shopping for a friend or family member or looking for a great white elephant gift for a party, we found the 58 best gifts under $50 for everyone on your list this year.

Stanley The Quencher 40 oz. Tumbler

Nordstrom

Staying hydrated is essential, and it can also be stylish with this cult-favorite tumbler from Stanley. This 40-ounce bottle comes in a variety of colors, keeps drinks warm or cold, fits in most cupholders, and is dishwasher-safe.

Brooklinen Interlude Candle

Brooklinen

Bedding brand Brooklinen is known for its ability to turn a bedroom into a comfortable, tranquil space, so it's no surprise that they offer an impressive lineup of candles — for an affordable price, no less. “This candle is part of my wind-down routine at home,” says Meghan Donovan, style expert and founder of travel planning service Paris, Perfected. “It has relaxing notes of calming guaiac and grounding patchouli. It's floral yet woodsy and warm, making it the perfect cozy candle for winter nights.”

Anthropologie Holiday in The City Mug

Anthropologie

This charming mug from Anthropologie depicts their favorite city. The mug has a gold handle and is hand-painted and glazed so each one is unique. Each mug holds 13.2 ounces and includes warm wishes with the city name printed inside the mug. 

Lego Harry Potter Hogwarts: Room of Requirement Set

Target

For any Harry Potter and Lego fan, you can’t go wrong with this set. The Hogwarts Castle showcases the Room of Requirement and includes 587 pieces, five mini character figures, and other themed accessories they'll remember from the series.

Latme Ice Roller

Amazon

This is the ideal gift for someone who would enjoy ice therapy to relieve face puffiness or head and neck pain. They can keep the small roller in the freezer or pop it in to chill for 10 to 15 minutes before use.

Sweet View Hair Brush Slick Back Set

Amazon

If you’re searching for a small gift or an extra stocking stuffer, this three-piece brush and comb set may be just what you’re looking for. The narrow bristle brush is a convenient size and is ideal for soft, fine, and baby hair, the rat tail comb works well for parting and separating hair, and the edge brush and comb is useful for keeping hair edges smooth and combing eyebrows and lashes.

Kendra Scott Maggie Huggie Earrings in Vintage Gold

Kendra Scott

These huggie earrings from Kendra Scott have a vintage look that’s super stylish. The hoops are made with 23K gold over brass and have a custom pattern. Plus, they’re small in size so they’re great for everyday wear.

Crate & Barrel Wood Marble Wine Cooler

Crate & Barrel

If you’re searching for an elegant gift for a wine aficionado, this marble and wood wine cooler from Crate & Barrel is a great choice. The simple and elegant design will fit in any decor, and the marble will keep wine chilled for hours.

Baimei Jade Roller & Gua Sha Facial Tools

Amazon

This self-care gift is a great way to show that you care. The set includes a double-sided face roller as well as a gua sha made of stone. They can be chilled in the refrigerator for an even more refreshing experience.

Crate&Barrel Tepi Throw Blanket

Crate&Barrel

This soft, two-tone blanket from Crate & Barrel makes a lovely gift for year-round warmth. It’s 70 inches by 55 inches in size and comes in six different color combinations. The extra-long fringe on both ends gives it a timeless style.

The Woman in Me

Target

The long-awaited memoir by singer Britney Spears details her rise to fame, tumultuous relationships, and nearly 14-year conservatorship. Any fan of the singer would appreciate the chance to read the complicated story in her own words.

Tarte Iconic Lashes Best-Sellers Set

Tarte Cosmetics

This set from Tarte Cosmetics includes three of the brand's most popular mascaras as well as a waterproof eyeliner. Whether they prefer products that offer length, volume, or curl, they’re sure to love this multi-product gift.

Ouai Chill Pills Bath Bombs

Sephora

These rose and jasmine-scented bath bombs will provide the ultimate luxury to your gift recipient. Each one contains jojoba, safflower seed, and hemp seed oil to moisturize and smooth skin.

Westman Atelier Squeaky Clean Lip Balm

Sephora

“This is a favorite gift of mine to give girlfriends year-round,” says Donovan. “It has such a great, non-sticky finish.” The clean formula delivers the lightweight hydration of a balm with the sheer tint of a lip stain, thanks to a blend of shea butter and jojoba, almond, and argan oils. Donovan is especially fond of the shade Nana for gifting, noting, “It’s a universally flattering, cool beige shade.” Plus, the packaging makes it feel especially gift-able. “It’s so luxurious that you don't even need to gift wrap it,” she says.

Pursoma Digital Detox Bath Soak

Violet Grey

For the person whose phone is constantly lighting up with pings, buzzes, and calls, treat them to this nourishing treatment — which essentially serves as a worthy excuse to sign off and unplug. The fragrance-free blend pairs French gray sea salt with French green clay to soften and smooth their skin while letting them zen out; the clay can even be used as a mini-mask to clear and gently detoxify skin. "I travel with these and love soaking in baths while I'm on the road,” says celebrity makeup artist Daniel Martin, whose A-list clients include Michelle Yeoh and Meghan Markle. It’s best used before bed to unwind the mind and body before tucking in for the night.

Handcrafted Studio Marble Bowl

Etsy

For a truly unexpected gift, you can't go wrong with this ruffled marble bowl. Grace loves it because it’s “so special and different,” she says. “It can be used as a catchall, fruit bowl, or just for decor! I love pieces that have a unique twist to a more traditional everyday item.” It's handmade from real marble, and you can customize both the diameters (which range from three to 18 inches) or opt for sets of two or three, which are available in various sizes. Whether they use it to display fruit or as a spot for keys on their console, it’s a guaranteed conversation starter.

Osea Salts of the Earth Body Scrub

Nordstrom

They can make quick work of rough knees and elbows in the shower with this body scrub, which is a fan favorite; it’s loved by beauty editors for its ability to gently smooth and soften skin with a blend of three salts from around the world (New Zealand solar salt, pink Himalayan salt, and red Hawaiian sea salt). At the same time, however, the shea butter and wild seaweed actively nourish and moisturize skin, leaving it feeling velvety and comfortable. “This body scrub is so luxurious, it feels like you're at a spa when you use it,” says Donovan. Its consistency keeps the shower mess-free, too.

Le Creuset Noël Collection Mini Cocotte with Star Knob

Le Creuset

This festive two-piece stoneware set from Le Creuset will be a holiday staple for your gift recipient. Available in red, green, or white, the lid of the glazed cocotte is embossed with evergreen trees and finished off with a gold star knob.

Drinking French

Amazon

The next best thing to a trip to Paris? This book. It features recipes for everything from liqueurs to spiced hot chocolate — Parisian-style, of course. It also includes snacks, which is par for the course for David Lebovitz, the author, American expat, and well-known pastry chef. “As the founder of a Paris-specific trip planning service, I'm a Francophile at heart — and this book is a favorite of mine to use at home as well as to give as a gift,” Donovan says. “It’s full of great recipes to bring France to your own rituals.” And, as expected, it comes with photos of the creations, which will have the lucky recipient restocking their bar in no time.

Tatcha Cedar & Hinoki Body Milk

Sephora

The Japanese-born concept of forest bathing (a.k.a. spending time in nature) has become popular in recent years for its ability to reduce stress, but it's not exactly feasible for everyone. That’s where this lightweight lotion comes in; inspired by the concept, it's infused with hinoki (a fragrant cypress native to Japan) to relax the senses. And with moisturizing ingredients like squalane and peach tree leaf extract, it leaves skin soft, supple, and thoroughly moisturized. “Finally, an after-bath lotion I can use from head to toe,” Martin says. “I love how lightweight this feels on the skin — but it hydrates and moisturizes like a cream.”

Brightland Awake Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Brightland

Olive oil is one of those things that few people are game to splurge on — and that’s what makes it such a great gift. Plus, it has a practical use. “I love a great olive oil and this one is my favorite to dress salads with. I mix this with fresh lemon juice and it complements anything,” Martin says. “It’s great for dipping, too!” It has a bold, rich flavor from the cold-press Arbequina olives, which are sourced from family farms in California. And the glass bottle isn't just for looks — not only is it UV-coated to protect it from oxidation (which makes it go rancid sooner) but it's also fully recyclable, too.

Intelligent Change Inc. The Five Minute Journal

Amazon

Whether you know for a fact that they subscribe to a meditation app or you've spotted Eat Pray Love on their bookshelf, take it as a cue to gift them this journal. “This is a thoughtful gift to give to the person in your life who loves to set goals and be reflective,” says Donovan. “It's a part of my at-home ritual for practicing gratitude.” With prompts intended to take just five minutes a day (hence the name), it also comes with inspirational quotes, weekly challenges, and space for daily affirmations. It's less intimidating than a blank notebook and encourages self-reflection ultimately making them happier and more grateful. 

Hydro Flask Travel Mug

Amazon

This easy-to-hold 24-oz. mug from Hydro Flask keeps hot drinks hot for up to 12 hours and cold drinks cold for as long as 24 hours. It’s also BPA-free, made of stainless steel, and has a convenient lid to keep drinks from splashing while on the go.

Artifact Uprising Walnut Desktop Photo Calendar

Artifact Uprising

This custom photo wall calendar comes in pine or walnut and has 12 printed calendar sheets with a different photo for each month. The calendar can be propped on a counter or hung up in a convenient location where it can be enjoyed all year long.

Cozy Earth Lounge Sock

Cozy Earth

Papier Make Your Mark Gift Set

Papier

If you know someone who loves to write, this is the ideal gift. The beautiful set is packaged in a classic box and includes a 192-page lined notebook, a rollerball pen, and a gold rainbow pen clip to keep the pen attached when not in use.

Artifact Uprising Modern Wall Tile

Artifact Uprising

These square wall tiles from Artifact Uprising make it simple to create a gallery wall that can be changed with ease. Each tile includes a printed photo as well as strips of self-adhesive for easy hanging without damaging the walls.

Amazon

This 20-oz. tumbler from Yeti is ideal for coffee to go. It has double-wall vacuum insulation to maintain the temperature, and both the cup and lid are dishwasher-safe. It comes in a variety of colors, so you can tailor it to your recipient.

Amazon

Anthropologie

This beautiful glass candleholder looks just like a cupcake topped with green, pink, or dark brown frosting. The candle inside features a different scent depending on the container. The green has a ginger cookie scent with cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and vanilla, the pink smells like a sugar cookie with hints of coconut, and the dark brown has the scent of caramel, coffee, and vanilla bean.

Papier

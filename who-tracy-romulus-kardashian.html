Who is Tracy Romulus?

Tracy Romulus is a businesswoman and the chief marketing officer of KKW Brands.

Tracy assumed the position in September of 2017.

She works alongside Kim Kardashian on the reality star's brands: Skims, KKW Beauty, and KKW Fragrance.

From 1998 to 2000, she attended the Fashion Institute of Technology for fashion design.

In an interview with People, Tracy said of her lifestyle with Kim: "Kim and I working together at that perfect moment in our lives was more than a decade in the making because we had been friends for so long."

The businesswoman continued: "What makes work so incredibly fulfilling for me is that I get to work alongside such brilliant, inspiring, creative women - like Kim and Kris Jenner - and the most talented, hardest working team in the world. I feel incredibly blessed."

Is Tracy Romulus married?

Tracy Romulus has been married to her husband, Ray, since 2014.

Ray Romulus is a music producer who has worked with the likes of Kanye West, Mariah Carey, Cardi B, and Justin Bieber.

Tracy and Ray are parents to three children: Ryan, Remi and Raf.

On December 27, 2021, the business guru posted a black and white picture of her and Ray.

She captioned the post: "7 years married today!!! Happy Anniversary to us @rayromulus."

Is Tracy Romulus on social media?

Tracy Romulus is active across social media.

She has over 495,000 followers on Instagram, and over 27,000 on Twitter.

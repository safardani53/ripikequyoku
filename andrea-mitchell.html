Andrea Mitchell Biography

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, June 6, 2024

Media personality, Andrea Mitchell is an American television journalist, anchor, reporter, and commentator. She is best known for her work with NBC News.

Andrea Mitchell's early life, married life, and divorce

Mitchell was born on 30 October 1946 in New Rochelle, New York. Her mother Sydney Mitchell was an administrator at the New York Institute of Technology in Manhattan while her father Cecile Mitchell was a chief executive officer.

Regarding her ethnicity and nationality, Mitchell is a white American journalist.

In 1970, she got married to a man named Gil Jackson in a beautiful wedding ceremony, after months of dating. But unfortunately, after five years of marital relationship, the couple got divorced in 1975.

On April 1997, she got married for the second time and her second husband is Alan Greenspan. He is an American economist who is a former Federal Reserve of the United States from 1987 to 2006. 

They started their affair in 1984 despite the huge age difference of 20 years after dating for about thirteen years they got married. Supreme Court Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg was the man who pronounced them as husband and wife. They are still together and happy having each other as a loving spouse.

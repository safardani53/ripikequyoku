Celebrities Wear Short Shorts: Pics Hollywood Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, April 24, 2024

Who wears short shorts? These celebs wear short shorts! Over the years, starlets across Hollywood have been rocking their favorite pair of high waisted shorts to perfection, donning them in creative ways for casual days out and about, music festivals, or even major concert tours!

A pair of denim shorts is a classic, staple piece of any wardrobe, and these stars seriously know how to work them. Plus, short shorts provide one of the best, clean slates for any type accessory or top that you want to wear. From dramatic flowing pieces, to graphic T-shirts, these stars know how to make a short short work.

Kendall Jenners Sweetest Moments With Nieces, Nephews: Pics

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, May 7, 2024

Although Kendall Jenner has yet to have children of her own, the model is surrounded by kids in the form of many nieces and nephews.

“Although I love my entire family — I love my nieces and nephews — this is definitely a massive birth control moment for me,” Kendall joked in a September 2022 episode of The Kardashians, when discussing sister Kylie Jenner’s second pregnancy. “Just because it is a lot.”

Since then, the Kardashian-Jenner family has continued to expand, and Kendall now has 11 nieces and 12 nephews. In addition to Kylie’s two kids with ex Travis Scott, Kendall’s older siblings, Kourtney Kardashian, Kim Kardashian, Khloé Kardashian, Rob Kardashian, Cassandra Jenner, Burt Jenner, Brandon Jenner and Brody Jenner, are all parents.

While Kendall loves being “Auntie Kenny,” she’s not rushing to have a baby of her own.

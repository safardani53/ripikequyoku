Callum Beattie reveals he spent 1,000 to give Calvin Harris a tape of his songs - and he still has

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 19, 2024

SINGER-SONGWRITER Callum Beattie revealed he forked out a small fortune to get his music into the hands of Calvin Harris.

The determined Scotsman turned a stag do with pals into a career opportunity as he headed to one of the DJ's gigs armed with his tunes.

Speaking to The Sun Online at V Festival, Callum explained: "I met Calvin Harris in Las Vegas one year. I went over there on a stag do.

"I put on my kilt and brought a few demos with me. I was still playing in the bars at the time. He said he'd give it a listen. It cost me $300 a ticket to get in and $1000 for a bottle of vodka but at least he got the demo."

However, it doesn't look like a collaboration between the pair is going to happen just yet.

When asked if he'd heard back from his fellow Scotsman, Callum said: "No, but I've got a couple of songs if he's interested [laughs].'

The We Are Stars singer also recalled the time he blagged his way on stage with R.E.M at T in the Park.

He explained: "I used a Hi-Vis vest to sneak in and was handing out my demo to all these rock stars. I was helping myself to all this lager backstage and I fell asleep.

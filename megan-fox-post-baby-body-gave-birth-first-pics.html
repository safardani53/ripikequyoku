Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Excuse us while we do a double, triple, quadruple take — Megan Fox had a baby on August 4, and she looked absolutely amazing while out on September 2! Megan was leaving a building in Los Angeles with husband Brian Austin Green and their newborn son, Journey River Green, and she looked so comfy and cool. CLICK HERE to see the pics! 

Brian did all the heavy lifting (as he should!) while they were hanging out in LA together with their youngest son, so Megan could relax and look chill in her casual outfit. It’s not as if Megan covered up in frumpy maternity wear during her pregnancy, but now that Journey’s four weeks old, we wouldn’t blame her if she were walking around in sweats with dirty hair!

Instead, Megan rocked an enviably casual look that works for weekday errands, or weekend hang outs. She’s dressed in tight black jeans, a loose-fitting grey tee, and a lightweight green jacket. Just a simple pair of flip flops and aviator shades are all she needed to accessorize the look. Well, that and a huge black diaper bag!

It’s so sweet seeing Megan and Brian with their baby. The couple actually filed for divorce in August 2015 after 11 years as a couple, five years of that being married. But they reconciled in the spring around the same time that Megan announced she was pregnant with their third child. The couple have two older sons, Bodhi and Noah; Brian has a child from a previous relationship.

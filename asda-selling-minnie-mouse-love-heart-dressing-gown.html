Asda is selling a Minnie Mouse love heart dressing gown, and it costs just 5

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, June 26, 2024

ASDA is selling a Minnie Mouse dressing gown with love heart print, and it costs just £5.

The bright red Disney cover-up, which is for kids, sold out online within hours of having its priced slashed from £8 yesterday - but will still be available in some stores.

George's new robe is patterned with white hearts, and has 3D ears on the hood.

With front pockets and a waist tie to fasten it, the bedtime favourite is practical as well as super cute.

The website description reads: "Wrap your little one up and keep them warm at bedtime with this adorable polka dot Minnie Mouse dressing gown.

"Complete with an applique Minnie Mouse design this item is a dream come true for any little Minnie Mouse fans."

It has got some very complimentary reviews online, including one mum who wrote: "Just lovely and super cute!

"My 8 month old looks totally adorable in this minnie robe, love the ears on the hood. Lovely snuggly and warm! Another great hit by George".

If you're anxiously waiting for the dressing gown to come back in stock, you'll be pleased to hear the supermarket is also selling Minnie Mouse PJs for girls.

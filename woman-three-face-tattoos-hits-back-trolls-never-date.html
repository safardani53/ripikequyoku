Woman who has three face tattoos hits back at trolls who say theyd never date her in blunt

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, May 31, 2024

A WOMAN with three face tattoos has hit back at trolls who said they’d never date her.

The 27-year-old showed off three inkings - one above her eyebrow and the other two under each eye.

On her @xlilsoup account she shared how she gets comments such as “urgh I would never date a woman with face tattoos”.

She said it also annoys her when people constantly ask the meaning behind the inkings.

The social media user hit back in a blunt but brilliant way, saying: “i got my tattoos for me and i certainly don’t owe you an explanation as to what they mean.”

Her video has certainly caused a stir online, and has racked up over 300,000 likes.

More on tattoos

Many people in the comments were full of support for her face tattoos.

One person wrote: “Your tattoos fit you perfectly.”

Another added: “I literally love everything about you.”

However, some viewers left a negative comment under the video, with one person saying: “ngl they look terrible.”

A second commented: “They do look terrible. Your choice though.”

This follows a woman getting her boyfriend's name tattooed on her forehead.

Though strangers online told her she was going to regret the ink, she says she's in love and will never be sorry about the decision.

TikToker Ana Stanskovsky (@anastanskovsky), who already has plenty of tats on her body, documented the tattoo process via social media.

"Getting my boyfriend's name tattooed on my face," she captioned the first video, which obtained more than 11 million views in one day.

She made several follow-up videos responding to criticism about getting Kevin's name permanently etched onto her face.

"Wow. You will regret this one day," one person said.

"Everyone was saying to me: 'I will regret that.' But every time I look in the mirror I'm in love," Ana answered.

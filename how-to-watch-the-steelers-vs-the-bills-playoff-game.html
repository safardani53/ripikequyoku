How To Watch The Steelers vs. The Bills Playoff Game

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, June 10, 2024
The stakes couldn't be higher. The Pittsburgh Steelers and the Buffalo Bills are scheduled to meet on Sunday, Jan. 14, 2024, at the Bills' home, Highmark Stadium in Orchard Park, New York, for a Super Wild Weekend NFL playoff game. The action will be presented live by the NFL on CBS.

The winner of the Steelers-Bills playoff game advances to the Divisional round, and keeps the team's Super Bowl dream alive; the loser goes home. Worth watching? We thought so.

The Steelers were 10-7 on the regular season; the Bills boasted an 11-6 record. Both the Steelers and Bills paved the way for their playoff matchup with big Week 18 wins. The Steelers made their case for the AFC's No. 7 seed with a 17-10 victory over the Baltimore Ravens. The Bills, meanwhile, clinched their fourth straight AFC East title -- and secured the AFC's No. 2 seed -- with a 21-14 win over the Miami Dolphins.

Who is Gina Lollobrigida's son Milko Skofic Jr?

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, October 6, 2024

ITALIAN actress Gina Lollobrigida, one of the biggest stars in the 1950s has passed away aged 95.

Gina is survived by her son Milko Skofic Jr, who she shared with her ex-husband Milko Skofic.

Who is Gina Lollobrigida's son Milko Skofic Jr?

Milko Skofic Jr is the son of Italian actress Gina Lollobrigida.

He was born on July 28, 1957, in Rome, Lazio, Italy.

Milko Jr is an actor, known for V.I.P.-Schaukel (1971).

He has been married to Maria Grazia Fantasia since September 1990, and together they have one child.

Did Gina Lollobrigida have any other children?

Milko Jr is the only child of Gina.

Despite being mother and son, their relationship was turbulent.

Prior to her death, she forced legal action against Milko Jr after he asked for control of her business dealings.

Milko Jr accused his mother's assistant Andrea Piazzolla of defrauding her.

Milko Jr told Italian newspaper Il Messaggero: "My mother needs an administrator - I fear she may no longer be capable of handling her affairs by herself."

In October 2022, he obtained a court ruling that meant she could only use her money for day-to-day expenses until legal cases against Piazzolla were resolved.

In a tearful interview on Italian TV that aired in November 2022, Lollobrigida said: "My legitimate right [is] to grow old without being robbed.

"I don't deserve this. I haven't done anything wrong, yet [my family] are furious against me and won't leave me alone.

"At my age, I should have some peace, but I don't have it yet. I have the right to live and also to die in peace."

Who is Milko Skofic Jr's father?

Skofic Jr is the son of Milko Skofic, a physician, manager and actor from Slovenia.

As his wife was a successful actress, Skofic decided to leave his medical career behind and became Gina's manager.

Skofic and Gina married on January 15, 1949, but separated in June 1971.

Despite all the actress's suitors, Gina never remarried.

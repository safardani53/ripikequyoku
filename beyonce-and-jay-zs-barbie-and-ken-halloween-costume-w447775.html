Beyonce and Jay-Zs Barbie and Ken Halloween Costume

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, May 29, 2024

https://www.instagram.com/p/BMQTZoXB5d-/

Come on Barbie let’s go party! Another day, another Halloween costume for Beyonce and her family.

The 35-year-old singer, her hubby Jay-Z and their adorable four-year-old daughter, Blue Ivy were plastic perfection on Monday, October 31.

The famous musical parents dressed as Barbie and Ken, with Blue Ivy made up up as a mini doll too.

Beyonce posted a few snaps of their impressive outfits on Instagram, and she quickly collected close to one million likes.

Queen Bey poured her curves into a black and white bodysuit, wore her hair in a high ponytail and sported Sunday Somewhere's Pixie Glasses. She’d stepped inside a "Black Barbie" box to complete her look.

Jay-Z, 46, looked very smart in a black tuxedo and silver bowtie. The “Empire State of Mind” rapper was also encased in a box with the words "Black Collector Edition" and “40th Anniversary Ken" written on it.

Blue Ivy rocked a black leather jacket with Barbie written on the back, fishnet stockings, a pink skirt and light-up Pop shoes.

The Knowles-Carter clan have barely been in regular clothes in the last couple of days. On Saturday, October 29, they hit up Beyonce’s cousin, Angie Beyince’s 80’s and 90’s themed party.

She recreated Salt-N-Pepa’s “Push It’ look with her daughter and her mom, Tina Lawson, while Jay-Z dressed as Dwayne Wayne from A Different World.

KJ Apa &amp; GF Clara Berry Celebrate Her Birthday With Sweet Message Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, February 12, 2024

KJ Apa isn’t particularly active on Instagram. But on December 7, the Riverdale actor, 23, made a rare move and shared a mirror selfie photo of himself with his girlfriend, Clara Berry, to celebrate the model’s 27th birthday! In the image, which was posted to KJ’s Instagram Story, the actor and Clara looked so loved-up, posing in front of a mirror in their cute and casual threads. The couple even matched some elements of their outfits.

Both KJ and Clara wore denim shorts with a length just above their knees. The torn fabric was a great detail of the garment that both young stars sported. KJ wore a graphic T-shirt while Clara donned a cute pink crop top. Even sweeter, the actor wrapped his arm around Clara while she embraced him from the side. “Happy birthday my love,” he captioned the image, along with two red heart emojis.

KJ and Clara are a notoriously private couple. Fans first became privy to their relationship back on August 19, when KJ took to Instagram and shared three images of Clara sunbathing — sans clothes — on a balcony. “There’s nowhere else,” he captioned the photos.

Since the impromptu August photo shoot, KJ hasn’t posted about his “love” on his Instagram Grid for months. Luckily, with the birthday message to Clara on his Instagram Story, it’s clear that these two are still very much in love and going strong. Clara, meanwhile, rarely posts images with KJ on her own Instagram, opting to give her fans a look at the various high-fashion outfits she models and more.

However, on September 25, Clara seemingly shared images from the couple’s getaway in August. While the photos featured the gorgeous model striking poses, she captioned the two images, “The look of my soul,” and gave photo credit to KJ! Before that post, Clara shared a series of three more photos of herself on September 13, adding the caption “C’est le chemin qui compte pas le résultat,” which loosely translates to “It is the path that does not count the result.” Once again, she gave photo credit to KJ!

Despite the fact that these two are so private, it’s always a treat to see their love flourishing. Fans cannot wait to see what the two share next. Hopefully, more mirror selfies of the pair and couple pics are in the future!

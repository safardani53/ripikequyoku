Sofia Vergara gasps in horror as fan takes savage swipe at her 'not perfect' face live on late-night

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, September 20, 2024

SOFIA Vergara has shared a throwback clip of a memorable moment during a late-night appearance.

The Modern Family star re-shared the moment on her Instagram Story, showing her on Jimmy Kimmel Live roughly nine years ago.

Sofia, 51, and host Jimmy, 56, were seen going back and forth as they read mean fan comments to each other.

The star looked sleek in a form-fitting red dress that seamlessly hugged her famous curves.

"Sofia Vergara has an amazing body but the face, not so much," Jimmy read aloud.

Sofia gave a shocked expression to the camera as the audience gasped, giving a quick sneer over in the host's direction.

The comment clearly got under Sofia's skin, as she read the next diss in a much harsher tone.

In the comments section of the throwback clip, re-shared onto a Modern Family fan account on Instagram, fans stepped in to defend the celeb.

"I feel so bad for her... so inappropriate. She’s beautiful and has a tremendous figure!" one person wrote.

A second said: "She was teary eyed at the end. This was awful to watch."

"Sofia is one of the most beautiful women of our generation," a third chimed in.

While Sofia shared a video from nearly a decade ago, she has been keeping fans updated on her life in real time as well.

On Christmas, Sofia went IG official with her boyfriend, Justin Saliman, at her family holiday party just five months after her divorce.

The America's Got Talent judge split from her ex Joe Manganiello, 46, over the summer.

The photo was taken at an intimate holiday dinner party, where Sofia took a selfie while wearing a silky green matching top and pants.

Her new boyfriend could be spotted sitting at the dinner table with the other guests behind her.

Justin is a successful orthopedic surgeon who has offices in both Beverly Hills and Los Angeles.

The source added: "Justin is absolutely charming, and handsome, and has an incredible job. He isn't an actor and is very confident and she is eating it all up."

They have been seen together on different occasions as the new happy couple have flaunted their new love on the streets of Los Angeles.

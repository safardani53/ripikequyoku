Made In Chelsea stars post the raciest snaps on Instagram and heres the proof

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 22, 2024

THEY might be posh, but the Made in Chelsea girls certainly aren't prudish when it comes to getting their kit off.

The reality TV babes all have huge followings on Instagram, and it's not hard to see why when you take a look at their saucy snaps.

From cheeky bikini shots to brazen bath pics, this lot know how to work it.

Here we take a look at the posts that earn the west London crew the title of raciest reality stars on social media.

Louise Thompson

The pint-sized Made In Chelsea star has been prolific on Instagram and Twitter over the years.

Louise, 28, joined the E4 show for its second series back in 2011 and is currently dating fitness fanatic Ryan Libbey.

His passion has clearly rubbed off on her judging by her recently super-toned limbs and six pack.

Frankie Gaff

Frankie, 23, joined MIC as Jamie Laing's potential love interest during series 11, back in 2016, and they went onto become boyfriend and girlfriend.

She left uni and is now a regular cast member - and frequent poster of sexy selfies.

Tiffany Watson

Tiff, 23, is the younger sister of long-standing former Made in Chelsea cast member Lucy Watson.

She may be taking a break from the reality series, but the gym buff blogger is certainly not shying away from the spotlight.

She recently posted a cheeky snap of herself in the bath. As you do.

Millie Mackintosh

Millie, 28, has become a star in her own right after leaving the show in series 5.

She's a fitness fanatic and makes fans green with envy with her sun-soaked Instagram bikini shots.

After a three year marriage to Professor Green, she's now back with fellow former MIC cast mate and ex-flame Hugo Taylor.

Kimberley Garner

Another Made in Chelsea veteran who, quite frankly, is more frequently spotted in a bikini than actual clothes.

But if we looked like that, we'd probably do the same as 28-year-old Kimberley...

Sophie Habboo

She might be a new kid on the block, but Habbs comes with an impressive Instagram following of over 44,000.

She recently broke up with fellow MIC cast mate Alex Mytton, though she doesn't seem too cut up about it...

Olivia Bentley

Liv, 23, only joined the cast in 2016 but has already caused her fair share of drama.

Being a fine art photographer who specialises in nudes, it's probably no surprise that she doesn't wear many clothes in most of her own photos.

Melissa Tattam

She's (allegedly) tamed Harry Baron, which deserves huge kudos (though we're not holding our breath on that one).

Having founded bikini brand MELISSAJAY, Melissa is naturally keen to model her goods...

Sophie Hermann

German-born Sophie first appeared in series nine but made a sensational return in 2017.

The "single and ready to mingle" star is also a fan of posting snaps from the tub...

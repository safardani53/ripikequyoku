Virgin Media TV customers receive huge free upgrade - check your box now

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, February 11, 2024

TELLY fans should look out for a free upgrade on their Virgin Media set-top box.

The broadcasting giant is making several channels available in HD to thousands of customers at no extra cost.

National Geographic HD, Sky Comedy HD and Sky Arts HD are among them.

Depending on the package you have, you could get up to 14 channels in HD.

Mix subscribers gain the most out of it.

Meanwhile, Mixit viewers get seven shiny HD channels upgrades, ITVBe and Film4.

Read more about TV

Customers won't need to take any action for the new channels to appear.

But you might have to reset your series link once the standard definition channel has been removed from Virgin 360.

The move means some of the channel numbers have changed too.

See the full list of channels and their number below.

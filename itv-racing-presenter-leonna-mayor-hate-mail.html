ITV Racing presenter and ex-jockey Leonna Mayor slams 'digusting, sad creature' for awful hate mail

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, January 28, 2024

LEONNA MAYOR slammed the 'digusting, sad creature' who sent her awful hate mail.

The ex-jockey, who is now part of the presenting team on ITV Racing, received the letter at Newcastle races ahead of the Racing League fixture on Thursday night.

The sad act who wrote it called her a 's***e gob' and said she had 'ruined ITV Racing'.

Mayor, who retired from the saddle in 2013 with 32 winners to her name and is the captain of Team Yorkshire, was appalled by the letter.

She is used to being in the public eye, having worked in broadcasting for several years while she was branded the 'world's sexiest jockey' after a lingerie photoshoot in Zoo magazine.

She has received plenty of stick on social media in that time, but the hateful letter was a step too far.

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, March 5, 2024

SIYA KOLISI looked shocked after his wife was hit in the head with a ball during South Africa's victory tour of Cape Town.

The Springboks were celebrating their Rugby World Cup triumph after beating New Zealand in the final in October.

During the celebrations, there was an unfortunate moment for captain Kolisi and his wife Rachel.

During the open-top bus tour, a fan attempted to throw a ball to the South African star.

However, the woman misjudged her throw, and instead of getting the ball to Kolisi, she instead hit Rachel in the head with the ball.

After seeing her blunder unfold in front of her eyes, the fan looked mortified.

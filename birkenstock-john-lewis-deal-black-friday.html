Shoppers can save 20% on Birkenstock Bostons at John Lewis in early Black Friday deal

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 23, 2024

BIRKENSTOCKS have long been a firm favourite for many shoppers.

While the sliders are usually synonymous with summer dressing, Birkenstocks' Boston Clog Slippers are perfect for autumn dressing and they've been a huge hit with fashionistas this year.

  • Birkenstock Boston Clog Slippers, £72 (Was £90) from John Lewis - buy here

We are not complaining, especially as Birkenstocks are currently on sale at John Lewis in its pre Black Friday sale.

John Lewis is offering up to 20% off shoes and boots in its Black Friday sale, which kicks off weeks ahead of the official 25 November date, and that spans across big brands, including the beloved Birkenstocks.

The mens Boston Clog Slippers have been a trend must-have for many months, as they can be dressed up or down, worn with tracksuit sets to run errands, or with jeans and a jumper for that everyday look.

While some may turn their noses up at the idea of socks and sandals, this shoe is the exception to the rule, as you can wear barefoot or with socks - we've seen all the fashion influencers do so.

The Boston Clog Slippers come in two colourways, Desert Dust Black, which is a light black hue, or Desert Dusk Gray that is more like a neutral sand colour.

This design features a traditional clog front to cover the toe area, with an open back to slide your feet into with ease.

It features an adjustable buckle at the front so you can tighten should you wish.

The upper shoe is made from Birko Flor Nubuck, while the sole is rubber, and has grip to ensure you are standing upright at all times.

The men's pair come in UK sizes 7.5 to 12, although sizes are selling fast.

Looking for womens Birkenstocks, you can see all John Lewis' deals here - but not all are on offer.

One top tip we have is to spray the shoes with a shoe protector, or Scotchgard, to protect the fabric from any stains or scuffs.

The best part is they have been reduced from £90 to £72 in the Black Friday sale.

How long the deal lasts this Black Friday - we don't know.

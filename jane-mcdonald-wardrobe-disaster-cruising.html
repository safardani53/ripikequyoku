Jane McDonald suffers wardrobe 'disaster' on new travel show Cruising the Med

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, April 24, 2024

JANE McDonald was left red-faced after she suffered a wardrobe "disaster" on her new travel show.

The Channel 5 star, 59, filmed her latest cruise show from the Mediterranean and treated viewers to a tour around Rome, Italy.

Jane was stunned by the art of Michael Angelo and decided to channel her inner creativity.

After taking part in a painting challenge onboard the cruise ship, Jane suffered a wardrobe malfunction as she spilt paint down her outfit.

After a day of sightseeing in Rome, Jane returned to the boat and opted for an afternoon of painting.

She said: "While we set sail west, I am choosing culture over the kitchen.

And joining in on the art classes, which is one of my favourite cruising activities. So what have we got to paint then?"

Another guest who was also taking part in the class explained: "It's going to be the Statue of David, you can emphasise whatever bit you want."

Jane quipped: "Oh ok, thank you very much, I might not even get down to that bit."

Jane showed off her painting skills and then began to look around at the other paintings.

Commenting on the woman next to her, Jane exclaimed: "Your is fabulous, yours is more dynamic!

"Yours looks like a man, mine looks like a waiter on a surfboard!"

But it wasn't long before Jane made a huge painting blunder

Accidentally dropping paint on her clothes, she said: "Sorry, it is all over my trousers! Despite my wardrobe disaster, I don't think I've done too badly.

"But then it's not my opinion that counts, is it?"

Painting teacher Gerri praised Jane's picture: "Look how nicely the red and the yellows are blending together."

Chuffed with the feedback, Jane joked: "Aww bless you, Gerri, if my voice ever packs up, I might have found a new way to make a living."

This isn't the first time Jane's outfits has caught the attention of viewers.

She recently defended her decision to wear skimpy swimming costumes on her latest Channel 5 travel series.

The TV star - who has hosted Holidaying with Jane McDonald this year - had jetted off to a number of sunny locations in the Caribbean.

Jane sat down with celebrity chef James Martin on his self-titled Saturday morning cookery show to chat about the series.

She fiercely defended her decision to wear skimpy swimsuits on the programme, where she was often seen dipping into the sea.

The singer said she "doesn't care" how she looks and insisted she wasn't bothered by criticism from people online.

As James, 49, cooked a delicious fish-based dish, Jane detailed her recent travels around the Caribbean, from St Lucia to Grenada.

"You say you don't like travelling but over there [the Caribbean], it's pretty special out there," James kicked off the chat.

"Well, I mean, I'm always on the go and I'm always away - but I've got such a great filming team and we have such a laugh," Jane replied.

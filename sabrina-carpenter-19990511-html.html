Sabrina Annlynn Carpenter is an American singer and actress. Carpenter made her acting debut with an appearance in the crime series Law & Order: Special Victims Unit and received recognition for her recurring role as a young Chloe Goodwin in The Goodwin Games. She had her breakthrough starring as Maya Hart in the Disney Channel series Girl Meets World. Carpenter has starred in the feature films Horns, Adventures in Babysitting, The Hate U Give, The Short History of the Long Road, Clouds and Emergency. She has also starred in the Netflix films, Tall Girl, its sequel and Work It. As a voice actress, she had a recurring role as Princess Vivian in Sofia the First, and as Melissa Chase on Milo Murphy's Law. In 2014, Carpenter was signed to Hollywood Records. Her debut single, Can't Blame a Girl for Trying and EP of the same name, were released in the same year.

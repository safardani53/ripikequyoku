Inside the 10-a-head SWINGERS nightclub boasting sex dungeons, a 'dogging car' and leather beds

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, June 23, 2024

THESE saucy snaps reveal a secret swingers’ club that boasts 43 rooms.

Wife-swappers at the Attic Adult Club in Derby can romp in a dungeon, maze and X-rated adult cinema.

The club has a replica indoor dogging area and a Fiat hatchback with the number plate DO99 ING.

A naughty schoolroom with a chalkboard and an antique desk is also used for orgies.

The dungeon holds a skeleton figure and kinky torture equipment for more sexy fun.

Horny punters are charged £15 per couple, £25 for single males and £10 for single females.

Hundreds of randy lovers are flocking to the club for theme-night bonk fests from across the Midlands.

SCHOOL-THEMED ORGIES

May's theme was tarts and vicars with June and July’s set to be "movie stars" and "school's out".

Other themes for the rooms include Aztecs and Valentine's Day with up to 500 customers filling the venue on a night.

Rubber sheets and a sex cage are part of the filthy fun at the club, which is tucked away in a business park.

Visitors are greeted by a member of staff and given a tour if they are new.

DARKENED ROMP ROOMS

Some rooms are pitch dark with windows almost entirely blackened allowing swingers to have sex anonymously.

Exhibitionists use a camping area with a large tent at the venue, which owner Ron Rothwell has run with since 2007.

He told Nottinghamshire Live: "The service we offer is somewhere safe and friendly where people can come and do their own thing.

“It's a safe and clean environment at all times.

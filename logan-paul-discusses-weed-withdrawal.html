Logan Paul Discusses Weed Withdrawal

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Logan Paul, the YouTube celebrity, has spoken out about his Christmas Day withdrawal symptoms after stopping using marijuana. The 26-year-old detailed everything for his 21 million followers in a message he posted on social media about his awful Christmas season withdrawal.

Logan Paul Discusses Weed Withdrawal

“I stopped smoking [marijuana] on December 24, and I’ve been suffering some pretty awful withdrawal symptoms (lack of appetite, sleeplessness, excessive irritability) – by far the worst week of 2021 for me,” Paul said on Instagram. His year-end problems, on the other hand, did not stop with his Christmas Day tribulations. In the same tweet, he stated that on New Year’s Day, he had food sickness and had to call an ambulance.

“I got food poisoning (which was unusual considering all I had for breakfast was a little croissant and a cappuccino),” he wrote. “I threw up 40+ times and sweated in bed all nite,” the YouTuber said. Logan Paul described how he couldn’t acquire any medicine to aid with his agony since all of the pharmacies in the vicinity were closed. Logan Paul had planned to enjoy the occasion with his buddies.

Paul requested that he be connected to an IV so that he could rehydrate his parched body and be done with the therapy. However, the medical professionals refused and stated he needed to be transferred to the hospital for additional care. Paul turned down their offer and continued with his strategy. The boxer managed to put on his suit and attend the party for 10 minutes before realizing his destiny was determined. Paul puked five times on the streets as he went back home, “defeated and sad,” unable to continue the celebration. “I didn’t skip a step,” Paul laughed about his encounter.

Is Logan Paul’s Year 2022?

Despite the fact that he has yet to get his salary from his June fight with Floyd Mayweather, Paul is hopeful that his start in 2022 indicates that things will only get better. “Having been physically and psychologically humiliated (again), the only way to go is up,” he added. This was not the first time the YouTuber has suffered from a major sickness during a New Year’s Eve party. He’d had identical problems in the previous two years. Paul refers to these occurrences as “involuntary resets.”

Despite the fact that he has yet to win a fight, the YouTuber-turned-boxer recently made ‘fighting redemption’ one of his New Year’s promises. Paul has said that he intends to return to the ring in 2022, for which he has given up cannabis in order to boost his training. To conclude the message, he wished his admirers a good new year and encouraged them. “I hope you all seize 2022 by the f***ing horns and do whatever you set out to do,” Paul said.

26 must-buys at Aldi this week starting at $2 - including cheap kitchen gadget that will slash your

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, May 28, 2024

ALDI has over 20 items in stores this week that will be perfect for preparing for the Super Bowl.

The large food market chain is selling kitchenware, clothing, and desserts for a cheap price.

Aldi is known for its affordable prices, but there are some items that are a must-buy this week.

A TikTok influencer, bitsbitesblog, also known as Katie Kelly, shared her top finds at Aldi.

As the Super Bowl is coming up in less than three weeks, people are gathering to watch the game and enjoy snacks.

Even if football isn't your thing, you can sit back and lounge with the products Aldi has in stock.

"Three-piece loungewear set comes with an eye mask," Katie said.

The store is also selling a ViSAGE Pro Style Heat Wrap for $24.99.

3. SERRA CARDIGAN

Another SERRA-branded clothing item is the cardigan, and it comes in two colors — pink and gray.

If you don't have anywhere to store your leftovers, this food storage set may be perfect for you.

Aldi is also selling baking pans in various sizes — squares, circular, and heart-shaped.

The Cast Iron Fry Pan by Crofton is sold for $18.99 for a 12-inch pan.

In comparison, Walmart is selling a 12-inch pan for $24.97.

8. 9-QUART STOCK POT

The 9-Quart Stock Pot is perfect for people who love making stews or soups.

It's large enough to cook almost anything, and it costs $26.99.

9. SUPER BOWL-THEMED PLATES

There are also Super Bowl-themed plates and napkins that are disposable.

The napkins have a football or a field on them, and the plates have a football design on the front.

Each item costs $2.29.

10. PIZZA PANS

Each pizza pan costs $4.99, and it comes in a couple of sizes.

You may want to check out the selection at Walmart first though, as we spotted pizza pans on sale for $1.98.

11. SERVING PLATES

If you're making guacamole, salsa, or any other dips, Aldi is selling a divided serving tray.

It only costs $14.99.

12. LIGHTWEIGHT CAST IRON

Another cast iron item is the Crofton Lightweight Cast Iron pot.

It is approximately 25 percent lighter than a traditional cast iron, and each one costs $29.99.

13. CAST IRON DESSERT SET FOR TWO

Making desserts in cast iron is more popular than you think.

Whether you want to make a pizza cookie or s'mores dip, this will get the job done.

The best part is that it comes with two cast iron dishes for $14.99.

14. DIGITAL AIR FRYER

Air fryers are pretty popular, and Aldi has two colors available for its customers.

One in white and one in black, and it is sold for $74.99.

In comparison, Kohl's is selling a similar-sized one for an even cheaper $67.99.

Meanwhile, popular brand Ninja charges $150 for its version.

Air fryers can help you cut your bills by reducing your energy usage, compared to an oven.

15. THREE-POT SLOW COOKER

For a night like the Super Bowl or any large gathering, cooking efficiently is a must.

The three-pot slow cooker can cook food in large quantities while you do other cooking or baking.

Each set is $39.99.

16. ITALIAN-STYLE MEATBALLS

Aldi has an organic dish of Italian-Style Sauce and Beef Meatballs.

It has 11 grams of protein and is made with organic grass-fed beef.

Each pack is just $7.99.

17. CLANCY'S CHIPS

The Super Bowl is not complete without some chips on the side to snack on.

Clancy's has delicious chip flavors like Buffalo Wings, Pickled Ranch, Bacon Cheddar, and Parmesan for just $1.99 per bag.

18. PRESSED JUICES

"I love this ginger juice," said Katie.

VitaLife carries different flavors of pressed juices like ginger and turmeric.

Each bottle costs $3.05.

19. CLANCY'S PRETZELS

Clancy not only has chips, but pretzels as well.

They come in two flavors — Garlic Parmesan and Bacon Habanero — and are $2.29 per bag.

20. MORNING RECOVERY SHOT

Hangovers happen no matter your age.

The morning recovery shot by More helps to relieve those groggy feelings.

21. DILL PICKLE POPCORN

A large bag of Dill Pickle Popcorn by Clancy's is just $2.29.

22. CHOCOLATE STRAWBERRY CHEERIOS

Cheerios were served at many schools, but this Chocolate Strawberry Cheerios won't be in stock for long.

HOW TO FIND THE DEALS

Aside from finding good food deals, clearance items at Aldi are worth checking out too.

All you need to look for is the yellow price tags because these are marked down for clearance and tend to be some of the lowest prices available.

There are also apps that offer special coupons at Aldi and dozens of other retailers.

Popular options include Coupons.com and Ibotta.

See what's on Dunkin's winter menu

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, June 12, 2024

YOU CAN snuggle up with your order from Dunkin' this winter.

Dunkin's winter menu will be back right in time for the season.

What is on the Dunkin' 2024 winter menu?

According to food insider Markie Devo, Dunkin's winter menu will introduce new donuts and drinks.

Many fan-favorite seasonal items will return, as well.

Lovers of the Brownie Batter Signature Latte will be disappointed to learn that their favorite drink won't return this winter.

However, the Pink Velvet Swirl Macchiato will be making a comeback this winter.

The macchiato combines espresso with red velvet cake flavor and cream cheese icing.

Also coming back is the Pancake Wake-Up Wraps, available with either sausage, bacon, or egg and cheese.

Speaking of bacon, Dunkin' is also bringing their Sweet Black Pepper Bacon.

The White Hazelnut Bark Coffee will make its debut this winter. The coffee will be available hot and cold.

Most read in Money

The new beverage will feature toasted white chocolate, hazelnut, and cream.

Another new winter addition will be the Frosty Red Velvet Specialty Donut.

The donut is topped with vanilla icing and cream cheese-flavored sprinkles.

When will the winter items be available to buy?

The winter items will be available at nationwide participating Dunkin' locations right on time for the cold season.

According to Devo, the winter menu will launch on December 27, 2023, just six days after the season officially starts.

Dunkin' will also release a few love-inspired items closer to Valentine's Day.

The beloved brownie batter donut will be back, as well as the Cupid's Choice donut.

The Cupid's Choice donut is filled with Bavarian Kreme and frosted with strawberry-flavored icing and pink sprinkles.

The Valentine's Day menu is expected to launch on January 29, 2024.

When does Dunkin's winter menu end?

Dunkin' has not yet released an official closing date for its winter menu.

The winter options are available for a limited time only and there is no guarantee that Dunkin' will bring your favorite options next year.

Depending on their popularity and supply availability, some items may be available for purchase all winter long.

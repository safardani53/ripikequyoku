Actress Maggie Grace is Married to Husband Brent Bushnell: Their Relationship, Dating History

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, June 7, 2024

Margaret Grace Denig, a.k.a Maggie Grace, is an American born actress and model, and she is currently married to her husband Brent Bushnell who is best known as the son of Nolan Bushnell. It has been quite some time now since they wowed the world with the news of their marriage. In fact, it has been more than 9 months since they got married back in 2017. 

Born on  21st September 1983, the 34-year-old is best known for her appearance as Irina in , also starring Kristen Stewart, Robert Pattinson, and Taylor Lautner. As of 2018, the actress is portraying the role of lead character Althea in TV series and she is also all set to appear as Casey in the film which will soon release in 2018. 

This is it for her career, as here we are going to share some information about Maggie Grace's marital life with husband Brent Bushnell and some of Maggie's past affairs.

Maggie Grace marital life with husband Brent Bushnell 

Maggie started dating her to-be-husband Brent Bushnell, the CEO of back in 2016. The couple tried to keep their affair away from the public but eventually, Maggie uploaded a picture of herself with her new beau sharing with the world that she was in a relationship.

Almost a year later, an announcement was made that the couple were engaged. They got engaged in February of 2017 on Valentines Day.  Little did everyone know that the couple was in a rush as just three months into their engagement, they decided to tie the knot.

On 28th May 2017, the couple got married in a ceremony which took place in La Jolla, California. According to , the pair's close friends and family members attended the wedding, meaning that it was a simple and small wedding without the glitz and glamor of Hollywood. 

It has been more than 9 months since they got married, and we are sure that they are still in their Honeymoon stage. Moving along, there are no rumors about the pair having a child but fans sure hope to hear the news very soon.

Likewise, there also are no rumors about the couple having a rough time after marriage, splitting or getting a divorce which is an excellent news. 

Maggie Grace past relationships and affairs. 

It might not come as a shocker to many but Brent is not the first man in her life. Previously, she has been in a relationship where she almost got married. 

The actress' first open relationship was with American actor Ian Somerhalder, best known for his work in . They started dating in April of 2006 and together they even adopted a cat named Roo. However, they don't have any child yet. 

Despite the serious commitment, they were ready to give as new parents to their new kitty cat, things did not work out between them. They broke up, a year later, in August of 2007. 

Following the break up with her first publicly declared beau, she started dating another American actor Blake Mycoskie. They dated from late 2007 to mid-2009. 

Finally, moving on to her infamous relationship with actor Matthew Cooke. They started dating in March of 2012 and dated for almost 3 years. After the time lapse, they got engaged in Feb of 2015. However, their relationship was limited to the engagement and did not proceed further to marriage. 

Maggie Grace with ex-fiance  Matthew Cooke

Grace and Cooke called off their engagement and announced their split just 11 months later in Jan of 2016. 

Despite the heart-wrenching split from the man who was in her life for more than 3 years, she moved on real quick and look what it has lead to now. She is happily married to a handsome man.

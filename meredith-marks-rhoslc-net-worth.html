Who is Meredith Marks from RHOSLC?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 20, 2024

MEREDITH Marks is one of the stars prominently featured on The Real Housewives of Salt Lake City.

Aside from her role on the controversial show, RHOSLC, Meredith has a thriving business that gives her a steady income.

Who is Meredith Marks from RHOSLC?

Born on December 15, 1971, in Chicago, Illinois, Meredith Marks is a celebrity jewelry designer.

She has a jewelry store on Main Street, Park City, Utah.

Before she got involved in the jewelry business, she earned a law degree from Northwestern University and subsequently pursued a Master's degree from Kellogg Business School.

She later worked as a real estate developer for some years. She also owned a health club for some years.

After she began admiring her great-grandmother's antique jewelry pieces, she decided to pursue a full-time career as a jeweler.

She also has a fashion line, and celebrities such as Rihanna have been seen wearing her designs.

What is Meredith Marks' net worth?

According to Exact Net Worth, Meredith Marks has an estimated net worth of approximately $1million, as of September 2022.

Her main source of wealth is said to be derived from her entrepreneurial career as a jeweler and fashion designer.

Is Meredith Marks Single?

Meredith Marks is currently married to Seth Marks.

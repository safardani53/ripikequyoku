Kylie Jenner fans gasp as they spot Timothee Chalamet in the background of Landons Barkers pho

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, January 29, 2024

KYLIE Jenner's fans spotted Timothee Chalamet in the background of her family's Christmas Eve party despite rumors they had split.

Her followers had become convinced that they had broken up recently after spotting a series of apparent clues.

However, Timothee, 27, was seen hanging out with Kylie, 26, on Sunday in the background of Landon Barker's post on Snapchat.

Landon had shared a photo of his dad, Kourtney Kardashian's husband Travis Barker, pulling a mock scowl at the lavish party.

In the background, two people that appeared to be Timothee and Kylie were seen standing close to each other and chatting.

The Wonka actor was in a black suit jacket and white shirt.

One eagle-eyed fan shared the photo on Reddit and wrote: "I spy with my little eye. Timothee at the Karjenner Christmas party."

Another added, "Honestly, cute," and a third posted: "Well well well."

Timothee and Kylie were confirmed to be dating in April, after being spotted together on multiple occasions earlier in the year.

However, earlier this month, The Kardashians star sparked rumors that they had broken up during an outing in LA.

Fans noticed that her phone lock screen had changed from a picture of Timothee to one of her kids.

Kylie previously had a picture of her and Timothee on her phone's background, where the actor was kissing Kylie on the cheek.

Now, however, Kylie's lock screen showed a picture of her children - Stormi, five, and Aire, one.

The Kylie Cosmetics star shares her kids with rapper Travis Scott, 32.

BREAKUP 'CLUES'

Days later, Kylie's best friend, makeup artist Ariel Tejada, fueled the breakup rumors by deleting posts of Timothee on Instagram.

Ariel had shared two photos of himself watching Timothee's new movie, Wonka, at the movie theater.

They showed Timothee on the big screen in front of him.

"@tchalamet you killed it!!!!! I'm definitely be watching!!" Ariel wrote in the caption.

However, the post was quickly deleted by the artist, raising speculation of a possible breakup.

Fans also speculated that Kylie had gotten the "ick" with Timothee.

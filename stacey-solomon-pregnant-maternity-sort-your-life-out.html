Stacey Solomon fans convinced theyve worked out wholl replace her on Sort Your Life Out

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, July 14, 2024

STACEY Solomon fans are convinced they've worked out who will replace her on BBC's Sort Your Life Out.

The Loose Women panellist, 33, revealed she was expecting her fifth baby in an emotional Instagram post last night.

The TV star will be taking a break from her usual hosting duties while she goes on maternity leave closer to her due date.

This means her presenting role on the BBC show is up for grabs.

Fans are convinced they know who will fill in for Stacey and the person in question has already starred on the last series.

Viewers think Stacey's good pal Sophie Hinchcliffe, also known as Mrs Hinch, will be the perfect candidate.

The show sees Stacey and her crack team help families transform their homes as they declutter, upcycle and recycle their way to a new home.

Mrs Hinch has become a household name for her cleaning hacks and home advice.

One fan speculated: "Ah is that the reason Sophie Hinch was working with her on Sort Your Life Out, perhaps taking over for her maternity leave?"

Another posted: "Mrs Hinch would be a great host on Sort Your Life Out."

Stacey announced her pregnancy in an Instagram video on Wednesday.

Sitting on the floor with a positive pregnancy test, the TV star shouted for husband Joe Swash, and told him: "Come in!"

As she waves the test in his direction he gasped and said: "What are you holding? You're joking."

His wife said "no" as Joe collapsed to the floor and gave her a hug.

Stacey previously stated that her daughter Rose, 1, would be her last child in an interview with You magazine in September 2021.

The former X factor star stated: "We have to live within our means. We want to be the best parents we can.

"And when we were trying to have this baby, it wasn’t the process we thought it would be, so we were just so grateful to even get pregnant.

"I don’t want to push it anymore. This is enough."

In October, however, when asked about having more children, Joe sang a different tune.

He told Fabulous magazine: "Having kids is addictive".

He went on to say: "I love it. I love being a dad and a husband. If I can be half what my dad was to us as a family, then I’d be happy.

"And I'm never going to say never to more kids. Let's see what happens."

Stacey has two older children, Zach, 14, and Leighton, 10, from previous relationships as well as Rex, 3, and Rose whom she shares with husband Joe.

Joe also has son Harry, 15, from his previous relationship with ex-fiancee Emma Sophocleous.

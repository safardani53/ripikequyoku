Who are the members of Mtley Cre and what are their net worths?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, March 2, 2024

HEAVY metal band Mötley Crüe got their start in 1980s Los Angeles.

The band has gone on to release nine studio albums.

Who are the members of Mötley Crüe?

Nikki Sixx

Born on December 11, 1958, Nikki Sixx is a bassist and songwriter, known as one of the co-founders of Mötley Crüe.

He's also been a part of groups Sister, London, 58, Brides of Destruction, and Sixx:A.M.

Nikki went on to launch the Royal Underground clothing line in 2006 alongside Kelly Gray.

Mick Mars

Born on May 4, 1951, Mick Mars real name is Robert Alan Deal.

He's known mostly as the lead guitarist and co-founder of Mötley Crüe.

Vince Neil

Born on February 8, 1961, Vince Neil is the lead vocalist of Mötley Crüe.

As a solo artist, he has released albums Exposed, Carved in Stone, and Tattoos & Tequila.

Tommy Lee

Born on October 3, 1962, Tommy Lee is known as the founding member of Mötley Crüe, as well as rap metal band Methods of Mayhem.

He has been married four times, and has two children with his ex-wife Pamela Anderson: Brandon and Dylan.

Their tumultuous relationship has become focus of the new series, Pam & Tommy.

Who are previous members of Mötley Crüe?

John Corabi

Born on April 26, 1959, John Corabi is a hard rock singer and guitarist.

Corabi acted as the frontman of Mötley Crüe between 1992 and 1996, while Vince Neil took a hiatus.

He's also collaborated with a multitude of musicians, including: Angora, Union, and Twenty 4 Seven.

Randy Castillo

Born on December 18, 1950, Randy Castillo was a drummer who worked with both Ozzy Osbourne and Mötley Crüe.

He died on March 26, 2002.

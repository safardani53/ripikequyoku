Why is Zoe Ball famous?

Zoe is a TV presenter and radio star, having become the first female host of the Radio 1 Breakfast Show in 1998.

She also co-hosted the classic Saturday morning kids TV show, Live & Kicking, with Jamie Theakston from 1996 until 1999.

Her dad is popular TV star Johnny Ball, who was a regular fixture in the 1970s and '80s on shows including Think Of A Number and Play School.

On January 14, 2019, Zoe took over from Chris Evans and became the new Breakfast Show host on Radio 2.

In May 2021, she announced she was stepping down as host of Strictly spin-off It Takes Two.

She was a contestant on the dance show in 2005, coming third with dance partner Ian Waite.

If you're interested in how tall Zoe is, she clocks in at 1.78m - that's 5 feet 10 inches.

Who is Davido's fiance, Chioma?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, June 1, 2024

DAVIDO is a popular Nigerian singer, songwriter, and record producer known for blending African elements with mainstream pop.

When Davido is not on stage, he can often be found at home spending time with his family, and now fans want to know more about his fiancée, Chioma Rowland.

Who is Davido's fiancée, Chioma?

Since 2019, Davido has been engaged to Chioma.

Chioma is arguably best known for her career as a chef and social media influencer.

She often posts recipe videos on YouTube to over 18,000 subscribers as well as content on Instagram to over 3.6million followers.

What happened to Davido's son?

The same year that they got engaged, Davido and Chioma welcomed their 3-year-old son, Ifeanyi.

Tragically, their son passed away on October 31, 2022, in an apparent downing incident at the family home in Lagos, the Associated Press confirmed.

At this time, it is unclear what happened, but police have since launched an investigation and have begun speaking to Davido's employees.

more from celebrity relationships

Since the news was announced, several fans and industry colleagues have taken to social media to offer their condolences.

“No parents deserve what they are going through right now,” Nigerian pop star Peter Okoye said of the situation on Instagram, via the Associated Press.

Peter Obi, a high-profile politician who is in Nigeria’s presidential election next year, also added that he cannot “begin to imagine the pain” that Davido and Chioma are going through.

"I send my sincere condolences to Davido and Chioma, over the death of their son Ifeanyi," he wrote on Twitter.

"I cannot begin to imagine the pains they are going through at the moment. May God grant them healing, strength and comfort in this trying time. My thoughts and prayers are with the family."

Neith Davido nor Chioma were home at the time of the incident, and they have not spoken publically about the death as of this writing.

What is Davido's net worth?

Due to Davido's successful career in the music industry, he has been able to amass a fortune over the years.

As of October 2022, his net worth is said to be $10million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.
